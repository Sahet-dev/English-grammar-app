may
might

A

Aşakdaky mysaly okaň:


Siz: Where's Bob?
Siz Boby gözleýärsiňiz. Hiç kim onuň niredeligini anyk bilenak, ýöne birnäçe takmynlar bar. He may be in his office. – Ol belki öz ofisinde bolmagy mümkin.
He might be having lunch. – Ol nahr iýip oturan bolmagy mümkinbr
Ask Ann. She might know. Enden soraň. Ol bilýän bolmagy mümkin.
May we might bir zadyň ähtimallygyny görkezmek üçin ulanylýar. Adatça may we might-y biri-biriniň ýerine ulanmak mümkin:
It may/ might be true. Bu odgry bolmagy ähtimal.
She might/ may know. - Ol bilýän bolmagy mümkin.

Ýoklyk formalary may not we might not (ýa-da mightn't) görnüşde bolýar:
It might not be true. - Bu dogry bolmazlygy ähtimal.

I'm not sure whether I can lend you any money. I may not have enough. – Men saňa karzyna pul berip bilmegim anyk bilemak. Mende ýeterlik bolmazlygy mümkin.

Gurluşy öwreniň:
I/ you/ he may/ might (not) be (true/ in his office we ş.m)
I/ you/ he may/ might (not) be (doing /working / having we ş.m)
I/ you/ he may/ might (not) (do/ know/ have/ want we ş.m)


B

Geçen zaman formasy may have (done) ýa-da might have (done) görnüşde bolýar.
A: I wonder why Kay didn't answer the phone. - Men haýran, näme üçin Keý telefona jobap bermedi.

B: She may have been asleep. Ol uklap ýatan bolmagy mümkin. A:I can't find my bag anywhere. Sumkamy hiç ýerden tapyp bilemak.

B: You might have left it in the shop. – Ony dükanda galdyran bolmagyň ahmal.

A:I was surprised that Sarah wasn’t at the meeting. - Saranyň mejlisde yoklygyna haýran galdym.

B: She might not have known about it. – Ol mejlis hakynda bilmän galan bolmagy ahmal.
Gurluşy öwreniň:
I/ you/ he we ş.m may/ might (not) have been (asleep/ at work/ we ş.m)
I/ you/ he we ş.m may/ might ( (not) have been (doing/ waiting/ we ş.m)
I/ you/ he we ş.m may/ might ( (not) have done/ known/ had' seen we ş.m

C

Käwagt could-yň manysy may we might-yň manysyny ýakyn:
The phone is ringing. It could /may / might be Tim. Telefon jyňyrdaýar. Bu Tim bolmagy mümkin.

Ýöne couldn't (ýokluyk formasy) may not we might not -dan tapawutlydyr.

Deňeşdiri:
She was too far away, so she couldn't have seen you. – Ol gat daşdady. Şonuň üçin ol seni gören bolmagy mümkin däl.

A:I wonder why she didn't say hello. Men haýran, ol näme üçin salamlaşmady.

B: She might not have seen you. -Ol seni görmedik bolmagy ähmal.
