will
shall (2)

A

Will kimdir biri geljekde etjegini eýýam meýil eden ýa-da karar eden iş-hereketi üçin ulanylmaýar:

Ann is working next week. En geljek hepde işlemeýär. (" Ann will work" däl)

Are you going to watch television this evening? -Bugün agşam telewizor tomaşa edersiňmi? ("will you watch" däl)

ýöne köplenç geljek hakynda gürrüň edilende gürrüň meýil edilen iş-hereket barada barmaýar.

Mysal üçin:
Do you think Kate will pass the exam?
Yes, she'll pass easily.

Chris: Do you think Ann will pass the exam - Seniň pikiriňçe En ekzaminda geçermi?
Joe: Yes, she’ll pass easily. - Hawa, aňsatlyk bilen geçer.

"She'll pass" - "Ol ekzaminden geçjegine karar etdi diýmegi aňladýar. Joe näme boljagy barada pikir bildirýär. Ol takmyn edýär.

Geljekdäki waka ýa-da pursat takmyn edilende will/won't ulanylýar.

Where will you be this time next year?" "I’ll be in Japan" - " Geljek ýyl şu wagt nirde bolarsyň? Men Ýaponýada bolaryn.

That plate is very hot. If you touch it, you'l burn yourself. – Ol jam gaty gyzgyn, ony elleseň eliňi ýakarsyň.

Tom won't pass the examination. He hasn't worked hard enough for it. - Tom ekzaminda geçmez. Ol onuň üçin köp taýarlanmady.

B

Adatça will aşakdakylar bilen hem ulanylýar.

I'll probably be home late today. – Bugün öýe giç gelmegim mümkin.

I haven't seen Carol today. I expect she'll phone me. – Men bugün Keroly görmedim. Onu maňa jaň ederine garaşýaryn.

I'm sure you'll pass the exam. – Men ynanýan, sen ekzaminden geçersiň

Do you think Sarah will like the present? - Seniň pikiriňçe Sara bu sowagady halarmy?

I don't think the exam will be difficult. Meniň pikirimçe ekzamin kyn bolmaz.

I wonder what will happen. Men haýran, näme bolarka.

(I) hope -den soň adatça present ulanylýar:

I hope Carol phones this evening. – Kerol agşam jaň eder diýip umyt edýän.

I hope it doesn't rain tomorrow. – Men ertir ýagyş ýagmaz diýip umyt edýän.

C

Adatça will geljak barada gürrüň edilende ulanylýar. Ýöne käwagt häzirki wagt barada gürrüň edilende hem ulanylýar:

Dont’t phone Ann now. I know she’ll busy. En-e buwagt jaň etme. Bilýän ol işli.

D

I shall.. / we shall..

Adatça shall diňe I/we bilen ulanylýar.

Deň I shall... ýa-da I will.., we shall... ýa-da we will... diýmek mümkin:

I shall/will be tired this evening. – Bugün agşam ýadow bolaryn.

We shall/ will probably go to Scotland or our holiday. - Biz dynç alyşa Şotlandiya barmagymyz mümkin.

Dilden aýdylanda I'll we we'll gysga formalary ulanylýar:

We'll probably go to Scotland. - Biz dynç alyşa Şotlandiya barmagymyz mümkin.

Shall-yň ýoklyk formasy shall not ýa-da shan't:

I shan't be here tomorrow. (ýa-da "I won't be..)- Ertir men bu ýerde bolmaryn.

He/she/it bilen shall ulanylmaýar:

She will be very glad. – Ol gaty begener. ("she shall be" bolmaz)
