prefer,
would rather

A

Prefer to do we prefer doing
"Prefer to (do)" we "prefer –ing-ler umuman nämäni artykrak(makulrak) görülýändigini görkezmek üçin ulanylýar.

I prefer something to something else. Men bir zady başga zatdan makul görýärin.

I prefer doing something to doing something else. (Bir zat etmegi başga bir zat etmekden makul görýärin.)

Emma: I prefer to do something rather than (do) something else.

I don't like cities. I prefer to live (va-da prefer living) in the country. – Men uly şäherleri halamaýaryn. Men obada ýaşamany makul görýärin.

Prefer-den soň gelýän gurluşlardaky tapawudy öwreniň.
I prefer this coat to the coat you were wearing yesterday. Bu paltony siz düýn geýip ýören paltodan makul görýärin.

I prefer driving to travelling by train. Otluda syýahat edenimden maşynda gezenimi makul bilýärin.

emma I prefer to drive rather than travel by train.

B

Would prefer (I’d prefer..)

"Would prefer" biriniň mälim pursatda (umuman däl) nämäni makul görýänini, nämäni isleýänini bildirmek üçin ulanylýar:

"Would you prefer tea or coffee? Coffee, please" "(Size bagly bolanda) Çaý içerdiňizmi ýa kofe? Kofe, haýyşt.

Would preferdan soň to... ulanylýar (-ing däl):
"Shall we go by train?" "Well, I'd prefer to go by car" - "Otluda bararymysmy? "Men maşynly barardym." (I’d prefer going" däl)

I’d prefer to stay at home rather than go to the cinema. – Kino gidenimden öýde galanymy makulrak görerdim.

C

Would rather (I'd rather..)

Would rather (do) = would prefer (to do). Would ratherdan soň to-syz infinitive ulanylýar.

Deňeşdiriň:
"Shall we go by train?

"I’d prefer to go by car"

"I’d rather go by car" ("to go" däl)

"Would you rather have tea or coffee?"

Coffee, please.- "Çaý içerdiňizmi ýa-da kofe?" "Kofe, haýyşt."
Ýokluk formasy "would rather not" görnüşde bolýar:
I'm tired. I’d rather not go out tonight. Men ýadow. Bugün agşam daşary çykmak islemezdim.

Would rather -dan soň gelýän gurluşy öwreniň:
I'd rather stay at home than go to the cinema.- Kino barandan öýde galardym.

D

I'd rather you did something

Bu gurluşy birinden bir zady etmegini isläniňizde ulanyp bilersiňiz.

"Shall stay here?" "I’d rather you came with us." - "Şu ýerde galaýynmy?" "Sen biz bilen gelmegiňi islärdim."

Bu gurluşda geçen zaman şekli (came) ulanylýar, emma many geçen zamanda däl, eýsem häzirki ýa-da geljek zamanda. Deňeşdiriň:
I'd rather cook the dinner now. – Nahary häzir eden bolardym (maňa bagly bolanda)

emma I'd rather you cooked the dinner now. Sen nahary häzir taýarlamagyňy islärdim.

Ýokluk formasy "I'd rather you didn't..." görnüşde bolýar:

I'd rather you didn't tell anyone about it. – Bu hakda hiç kime aýtmazlygyňy islärdim.
