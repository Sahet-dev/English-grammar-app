Başga birine Polyň aýdanlaryny aýdyp bermekçi bolýarsyňyz.
Munuň iki ýoly bar:
Siz Polyň aýdanlaryny gaýtalamagyňyz mümkin. (güriň özleşdirme) (direct speech):

Paul said "I'm feeling ill". - "Men özümi erbet duýýaryn” diýdi Pol.

Ýa-da siz göçürme gürrüňden(reported speech) peýdalanmagyňyz mümkin:

direct: Paul said am feling ill.

B

Reported speech -i ulanýanymyzda esasy işlik adatça geçen zaman şeklinde bolýar.
(Tom said that.../ I told her that... we ş.m). Sözlemň beýleki bölegi hem geçen zamanda bolýar:

Tom said that he was feeling ill. - Tom özini hassa duýýandygyny aýtdy.

I told her that I didn't have any money.- Oňa hiç hili pulum ýokdygyny aýtdym.

Gürrüňde that-y düşürip galdyrmak hem mümkin:
Tom said (that) he was feeling ill. I told her (that) I didn’t have any money.

Umuman garalanda, direct speech-däki häzirki zaman şekli reported speech -de geçen zamana öwrülýär:
am/is --> was
do/does --> did
will--> would
are --> were
have/has--> had
can--> could
want/like/know/go we ş.m wanted/liked/knew/went we ş.m

Direct speech we reported speech –leri deňeşdiriň:
"My parents are very well"-"Meniň ene-atam gaty gowy". Judy said that her parents were very well. Judi onuň ene-atasy gaty gowydygy aýtdy.

I’m going to learn to drive. - Men maşyn sürmäni öwrenmekçi. Jenny said that she was going to learn to drive. Jeni maşyn sürmäni öwrenjekdigini aýtdy.

"John has given up his job" - "Jon işini taşlady".

Michael said that John had given up his job. Maýkl Jonyň işini taşlandygyny aýtdy.

"I'Il phone you tonight" - "Bugün agşam saňa jaň ederin". Dennis said she would phone me tonight. Dennis bugün agşam saňa jaň etjekdigini aýtdy.

"I don't know where to go" - "Nirä gitjegimi bilmeýärin". Jack said that she didn't know where to go. Jek onuň nirä gitjegimi bilmeýädigini aýtdy. "I cant come on Friday." - "Men anna güni gelip bilmeýärin". Mary said she couldn't come on Friday." Meri anna güni gelip bilmeýändigini aýtdy.

Işligiň past simple şekili (did/saw/knew we ş.m) adatça reported speech-de özi galmagy mümkin, ýa-da ony past perfect şekline (had done/ had seen / had known we ş.m) üýtgetmegi mümkin:
direct speech
Tom said: "I woke up feeling ill, so l didn't go to work, "Men özimi hassa duýup oýanypdym we şonuuň üçin işe barmadym” diýdi Tom.
reported speech
Tom said (that) he woke up feeling ill, so he didn't go to work.
Ýa-da Tom said (that) he had woken up feeling ill, so he hadn't gone to work. Tom özini hassa duýup oýanypdygyny we şonuň üçin işe barmadygyny aýtdy.
