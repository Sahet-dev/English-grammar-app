verb + -ing,
verb + to
(like, would like)

A

like
love
hate
enjoy
mind
dislike
can't bear
can't stand

Aşakdaky işlikleriň hemmesi halamak ýa-da halamazly manylaryy berýär. Adatça olar soň gelen işlikere -ing goşulýar:

Ann hates flying. –En uçmany ýaman görýär.

Why do you dislike living here?- Näme üçin bu ýerde ýaşamany halamaýarsyň?

I don't like people shouting at me. – Maňa gygyrýan gepleýän adam halamaýary.

Love, hate we can't bear -lerden soň to-ny hem ulnmak mümkin:
I love meeting people. Ýa-da I love to meet people. -Adamlar bilen duşuşmany gowy görýärin.

She can't bear being alone. Ýa-da She can't bear to be alone. – Ol ýalňyzlyga çydap bilmeýär.

Emma enjoy/ dislike /mind / can't Stand- lardan soň diňe -ing ulanylýar:

I enjoy being alone. – Ýalňyz bolmany gowy görýärin. (I enjoy to be" däl)

Tom doesn't mind working at night. – Tom gijelerne işleýänine nägile däl. ("mind to work" bolmaýar)

B

Like

"I like doing something" we "I like to do something" deň ulanylýar:
I like getting up early. Ýa-da I like to get up early.- Men ir turmany gowy görýärin

Britan iňlis-dilinde "I like doing" we I like to do" -laryň araasynda azajyk tapawut bar.

"I like doing something"- "Men bir iş etmäni gowy görýärin” diýen manyny berýär.

Do you like cooking? - Nahar bişimäni gowy görýärsiň?

I like living here. - Bu ýerde ýaşamany halaýaryn.

"I like to do something"- "Men bir iş etmegi halaýaryn, çünki şu işi etmek dogry ýa-da gowy bolýar” diýen manyny aňlatýar.

I like to clean the kitchen as often as possible. Men aşhanany mümkin boldugyça ýygy-ýygydan arassalamany gowy görýärin. (Munda "arassalamak pursadyndan rahatlanýaryn" diýen manyny dälde, eýsem “ýygy-ýygydan arassalamak gowy iş” diýen many nazarda tutylýar.)

Mary likes people to be on time. Merä wagtynda gelýän adamlar ýaraýar. (Çünki bu dogry we gowy häsiýet.)

C

Would like/ would love/would hate /would prefer-lerden soň adatça to.. ulanylýar: I would like to be rich. - Men baý bolmak islärdim.
I'd love (= would love) to be able to travel round the world. - Dünýä künjeklerne syýahat edip bilmegimi islärdim.

Would you prefer to have dinner now or later?- Nahary häzir edinerdiňizmi ýa soňrak?

I like we I would like -lary deňeşdiriň:
I like playing / to play tennis. Men tenis oýnamany halaýaryn.

I would like to play tennis today. - Men bugün tenis oýnamagy islärdim.

Would mind-dan soň -ing (to... däl) ulanylýanyny ýatda saklaň.

Would you mind closing the door, please? Gapyny ýapyp goýberersiňizmi, haýyşt?

D

I would like to have done something diýip aýdylma bar. Onuň manysy bir işi (geçmişde) eden bolmagymy isleýärin (ol işi etmedim ýa-da edip bilmedim).

It's a pity we didn't see Val when we were in London. I would like to have seen her again. Bu gynançly , Londonda bolanymyzda Veli görmedik. Ony (şol wagtda) ýene gören bolsadym.

We'd like to have gone on holiday but we didn't have enough money. Dynç alyşa giden bolmagymyzy islärdim.

We'd like to have gone on holiday but we didn't have enough money. - Biz dynç alyşa giden bolmagymyzy islärdik, ýöne pulumyz ýeterlik däldi. Bu birikmeleri would love/would hate/ would prefer lerden keyin hem ulanmak mümkin:

Poor old Tom! I would hate to have been in his position. Garry biçäre Tom! Onuň ornunda bolygy islemezdim.
