A

Aşakdaky mysallary öwreniň:

Annanyň eşigi reňkler bilen hapalanan.

She has been painting the ceiling. –Ol üçegi reňkläp ýördi.
Has been painting - present perfect continuous.

Bizi iş-herektiň özi gyzyklandyrýar onuň gutarandygy ýa-da gutarmandygynyň ähmiýeti ýok. Bu mysalda iş-herekt gutaran.

Üçek akdy. Häzir bolsa ol gyzyl.

She has painted the ceiling. – Ol üçegi boýady.
Has painted - present perfect.

Munda iş-herektiň gutarnykly bolmagy möhüm. "Has painted" – tamamlanan iş-herket. Bizi iş herketiň özi dälde onuň netijesi (reňklenen üçek) gyzyklandyrýar.

Continuous henizem dowam edip gelýän iş-herketiň haçandan bäri dowam edip gelýänini görkezmek üçin ulanlyýar.

Simple how much, how many ýa-da how many times-lar berýän manyny aňlatmak üçin ulanylýar. Munda iş-herekt gutarnykly.

How long have you been reading that book?
- Sen bu kitaby haçandan bäri okaýň?

How many pages of that book have you read? / Bu kitabyň näçe sahypasyny okadyň?

Mary has written ten letters today. / Meri bugün on sany hat ýazdy.

They've been playing tennis since 2 o'clock. / Olar sagat 2den bäri tenis oýnaýarlar.

They’ve played tennis three times this week.
– Olar şu hepdäniň içinde üç gezek tenis oýnadylar.
