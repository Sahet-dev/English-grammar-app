have
vs
have got

A

"Have we have got " eýe bolmak, eýelik etmek

Köp ýagdaýlarda have-iň ýerine have got ulanylýar:

We've got a new car. ýa-da We have a car. - Biziň maşynyz bar.

Ann has got two sisters. Ýa-da Ann has two sisters. Eniň iki dogany bar.

Have got ýa-da have nähilidir kesel, agyry we suňa meňzeşler üçin hem ulanylýar.

I've got a headache. Ýa-da I have a headache. -Kellam agyrýar.

Sorag ýa-da ýokluk gürrüňleriň ýasalyşy 3 hili bolup bilýär:
Have you got any money?
Do you have any money?
Have you any money? (az ulanylýar) - - Pulyň barmy?

I haven't got any money.
don't have any money.
I haven't any money. - (az ulanylýar) - Pulum yok.

Has she got a car?
Does she have a car?
Has she a car? - (az ulanylýar) Onuň maşyny barmy?

She hasn't got a car.
She doesn't have a car.
She hasn't a car. - (az ulanylýan) - Onuň maşyny ýok.

Have " eýe bolmak, eýelik etmek" manysynda Gelen ýagdaýda, continuous formasynda (is having / having) ulanylmaýar:

I have/'ve got a headache. – Kellam agyrýar . ("l'm having" bolmaýar)

Geçen zaman formasy üçin had ulanylýar (adatça got-syz):

Ann had long hair 2 years ago. Eniň iki ýyl ozal uzyn saçy bardy. ("had got" däl)

Geçen zamanyň ýoklygy edip did didn't –dan peýdalanylýar:

Did they have a car when they were living in London? -Londonda ýaşanlarynda olaryň maşyy barmydy?

I didn't have a watch, so I didn't know the time. Mende sagat ýokdy we şeýdip wagty bilmeýärdim.

Ann had long hair, didn't she? – Eniň uzyn saçy bardy, şeýlemi?

B

Have breakfast / have a bath / have a good time we ş.m

Have (have got däl) iş-herekt we tejribeleri beýan edýän söz birikmelerinde köp duş gelýär. Mysal üçin:
have breakfast/ dinner/a cup of coffee/a cigarette we ş.m
have a bath /a shower / a swim / a rest/ a party/ a holiday/ a nice time we ş.m
have an accident/an experience/a dream we ş.m
have a look (at something) / a chat (with somebody)
(= çaga dogurmak)
have difficulty/ trouble / fun

Mary had a baby recently. - Meri ýakynda çaga dogdy.

Have a look at that building! How beautiful! Hana ol bina seret nähili owadan.!

Aşakdaky mysallarda have got –y ulanmak dogry däl. Deňeşdiriň:

I usually have a sandwich for my lunch. Adatça abeda men sendwiş iýýän. (have = iýmek, have got = iýmek däl)

emma l've got some sandwiches. Would you like one?- Mende birnäçe sendwiş bar. Birini bereýenmi?

Bu mysallarda have başga işlikler ýaly hyzmat edýär. Şonuň uçin gerekli bolan ýerleri üçin continuous formada (is having / are having we ş.m) ulanmak mümkin:

The phone rang when we were having dinner. - Biz nahar iýip otyrkak telefon jyňyrdapdy.

Sorag we ýokluk sözlemler adatça do/ does/ did -lar ulanylýar.

I don't usually have a big breakfast. – Adatça men ertirlik nahara köp zat iýmeýärin. ("I usually haven't" däl)

What time does Ann have lunch? – En haçan günortanlyk nahar edinýär? ("has Ann lunch bolmaýar) Did you have any difficulty finding somewhere to live?- Ýaşamaga bir ýerler tapmakda kynçylyklara duş geldiňmi?
