if i did

A

Aşakdaky mysallary deňeşdiriň:
1 Syu sagadyny ýitirdi. Onuň pikirine görä sagat Eniň öýünde bolmagy mümkin.
Syu: I think I left my watch at your house. Have you seen it? – Meniň pikirimçe sagadymy, seniň öýüňde galdyrdym. Ony görmediňmi?
Anna: No, but l'll have a look when I get home. If I find it, I'll tell you. Ýok emma ony gözläp görerin. Eger ony tapsam saňa aýdaryn.

Bu mysalda sagady tapmak ähtimallygy ýokary bolany üçin En If I find..., I'll.. diýýär.

(2) Anna: If I found a wallet in the street, I'd take it to the police. Eger köçede gapjyk tapyp alsam ony polisiýa bererdim. (ýa-da tabşyrardym)

Bular dürli pursatlar. Munda En köçede gapjyk tapyp almak ähtimallygy ýokary diýip hasaplanmaýar.

Ol şeýle pursady göz öňüne getirýär we köçede gapjyk tapmagyna garaşmaýar. Şonuň üçin ol "If I found., Id (= would) diýýär (If I find. I will däl).

Ýokardaky ýaly göz öňüne getirenimizde , if +past (if I found/ if you were/ if we didn't we ş.m) gurluş ulanylýar. Emma manysy geçen zamanda däl:

What would you do if you won a million pounds? Million funt utsaň näme ederdiň? (biz onuň hakykatddan hem bolup geçmegine garaşmaýarys)

I don't really want to go to their party, but probably will go. They'd be offended if I didn't go. Men olaryň oturlyşygyna gitmek islemeýärin, emma men giderin. Barmasam olar öýkelärler.

B

Sözlemiň if-bölümindde adatça would ulanylmaýar:

I'd be very frightened if somebody pointed a gun at me. – Biri maňa tüpeň çenese gorkan bolardym/ gorkýaryn. ("if somebody would point... däl)

Ýöne "if... would" guruluşy birinden bir etmeegi haýyşt edilende ulanylmak mümkin:
(resmi hatda) I would be grateful if you would send me your brochure as soon as possible. Broşýuraňyzy gysga wagtda iberseňiz minnetdar bolardym.

C

Sözlmeiň başga bölüminde (if-bölüminde däl) would ('d)/wouldn't ulanylýar:
If you took more exercise, you'd (= you would) probably feel healthier. Eger köpräk türgenleşseň özüňi sagdynrak duýarsyň.

Would you mind if I used your phone? Telefonyňdan peýdalansam bolarmy?

I'm not tired enough to go to bed yet. I wouldn't sleep (if I went to bed now). – Men heniz ýerime geçip ýatar ýaly ýadow däl. (Häzir ýatsam.) Uklamaryn

Beýle ýagdaýlarda could we might-y hem ulanmak mümkin:
If you took more exercise, you might feel healthier. - Eger köpräk türgenleşseň özüňi sagdynrak duýarsyň.

If it stopped raining, we could go out. Eger ýagyş dynsa köçä çykardyk (çykyp bilerdik).

D

Şu unitdäki mysallarda when ulanylmaýar:
They would be offended if we didn't accept their invitation. Olaryň teklibini kabul etmesek olar öýkelärler. ("when we didn't" däl)

What would you do if you were bitten by a snake?-Seni ýylan çaksa näme ederdiň / edersiň? ("when you were bitten" däl)

If we when-ler şeýlede Unit 25 C den gaýta görüň.
