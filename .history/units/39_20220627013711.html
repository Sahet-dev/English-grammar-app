if i had known ...
i wish i had known ...

A

Aşakdaky mysallary öwrreniň:
Geçen aý Garri hassahanada ýatypdy. Liz muny bilmändi, şonuň üçin ony görmäne barmady. Birnäçe gün ozal olar duşuşdylar.

Liz Gari-i diýdi:
If had known you were in hospital, Iwould have gone to visit you. Seniň hassahanada bolanyňy bilýän bolanymdadym, seni görmäge baran bolardym.

Liz "If I had known..." diýdi. Ol Gariniň hassahandadygyny bilmändi.

Geçen zaman barada gürrüň edilende if + had (d) (if I had known/been/done we ş.m) gurluşy ulanylýar:
I didn't see you when you passed me in the street. If l'd seen you, of course I would have said hello. –Köçeden geçip gideniňi seni görmändim. Seni gören bolanymda elbetde saňa salam berren bolardym. (emma seni görmedim)

The view was wonderful. If I’d had a camera, I would have taken some photographs. - Görnüş ajaýypdy. Egerde fotoaparadym bar bolanda birnäçe surata alardym. ( emma fotoaparadym ýokdy)

Deňeşdiriň:
I'm not hungry. If I was hungry, I would eat something. – Men aç däl. Men ajygan bolanymda bir zatlar iýerdim/ iýerin.(häzir)

I was not hungry. If l had been hungry, would have eaten something. – Men aç däldim. Men aç bolýan bolamda bir zatlar iýen olardym.(geçmişde)

B

Sözlemiň if-bölüminde would-y ulanmaň, would sözlemiň ikinji bölüminde ulanylýar:
If l had seen you, I would have said hello.
Seni gören bolsadym saňa salam beren bolardym. (if l would have seen you" däl)

Ýatda saklaň:
'd- would-yň ýa-da had-iň gysga formasy bolmagy mümkin.

If I'd (= had) seen you, I'd (= would) have said hello. Seni gören bolsadym saňa salam beren bolardym.

C

Şu usulda wish-den soňra hem had (done) ulanylýar. I wish something had happened - " Käşgä bir zat bolan bolsady, bir zat bolmagyna gynanýaryn.

I wish l'd known that Gary was ill. I would have gone to see him. Hany indi Garriniň hassadygyny bilýän bolsadym, men Garrini görmäne giden bolardym.(emma bilmeýärdim)

Do you wish you had studied science instead of languages? – Dil ornuna başga ylymlary okan bolmagyňy isleýärsiiňmi? (başga ylymyň ýerine dil öwreneniňe ökünýäsiňmi) (sen ylym öwrenmediň)
:bular ýaly gürrüňlerde wish-den soň would have.. ulanylmaýar.

I wish it had been warmer. Käşgä bu ýylyrak bolan bo'lsa. ("I wish it would have been däl)

D

Would (do) we would have (done)-ny deňeşdiriň.

If I had gone to the party last night, I would be tired now. - Düýn agşam oturylyşyga giden bolsam, şuwagt ýadow bolan bolardym. (häzir men ýadow däl, Häzirki zaman)

If I had gone to the party last night, I would have met lots of people. Düýn agşam oturylyşyga giden bolsam, köp adamlara duşan bolardym. (men köp adamlar bilen duşuşmadym – geçen zaman)

Would have, could have we might have-leri deňeşdiriň:

If the weather hadn’t been so bad, we would have gone out. - Howa beýle erbet bolmadyk bolsa, biz çykardyk.

If the weather hadn't been so bad, we could have gone out. Howa beýle erbet bolmadyk bolsa, biz çykyp bilen bolardyk.

If the weather hadn't been so bad, we might have gone out. Howa beýle erbet bolmadyk bolsa, belki biz çykardyk.
