must
mustn't

A

Must mustn't needn't

"You must do something" - "bir zady etmegi zerur/ şert/ gerek/ ":
Don't tell anyone what I said. You must keep it a secret. Aýdanlarymy hiç kime ýatma. Ony syr saklamagyň zerur.

We haven't got much time. We must hurry. Biziň wagtymyz köp däl. Biz howlukmagymyz zerur.

"You mustn't do something" = "Sen bir zady etmezligiň zerur. (şonuň üçin ony etme)"

You must keep it a secret. You mustn't tell anyone else. – Sen muny syr saklamagyň zerur. Sen ony başga birine aýtmazlygyň zerur.

It's essential that nobody hears us. We mustn't make any noise. Bizi hiç kim eşitmezligi gaty möhümdir. Goh etmezligimiz zerur.

"You needn't do something" = "sen bir işi etmegiň zerur däl

You can come with me if you like but you needn't come if you don't want to. Sen isleseň meniň bilen naryp bilersiň, ýöne islemeseň gitmegiň zerur däl.

We've got plenty of time. We needn't hurry. Wagtymyz ýeterlik. Howlukmagymyzy zerur däl.

B

Needn't –yň ornuna don't/doesn't need to-ny ulanmak mümkin.
We needn't hurry. Ýa-da We don't need to hurry. –Howlukmagymyz zerur däl.
Ýatlatma:
"don't need to do" (to bilen) Emma "needn't do" (to-syz)
Needn't we don't need to-laryň manysy don't have to –nyňka meňzeýär. (Unit 31 C):
We've got plenty of time. We don't have to hurry.- Wagtymyz köp. Howlukmagymyz zerur däl.

C

Needn't have (done)

Aşakdaky mysaly öwreniň:
Jorj daşary çykmaly bolupdy. Ol ýagyş ýagar diýip oýlapdy, şonuň üçin ol saýawan almana karar edipdi.
I needn't have brought the umbrella.
Emma ýagyş ýagmady we saýawan hem şert bolmady. Diýmek:
He needn't have taken the umbrella. – Onuň saýawan alyp çykmagy zerur däldi.

Needn't (do) we needn't have (done)-lary deňeşdiriň:

That shirt isn't dirty. You needn’t wash it. - Bu köýnek kirdäl. Ony ýuwmagyň zerur däl.

Why did you wash that shirt? It wasn't dirty. You needn't have washed it. Bu köýnegi näme üçin ýuwduň? Ol kir däldi. Ony ýuwmagyň zerur däldi.

D

Didn't need to (do) we needn't have (done)

I didn't need to do something – Men bir işi etmegim zerur däldi (we men muny şol bilýärdim)

I didn't need to get up early, so I didn't. Wagtly trumagyň zerur däldi şonuň üçin turmadym.

I didn't need to get up early, but it was a lovely morning, so I did. – Ir trumagyň zerur däldi, ýöne daň juda ajaýypdy, şonuň üçin turdym.

I needn't have done something- men iş etdim, emma ony etmegim zerur bolmandyny häzir bilýärin.

I got up very early because I had to get ready to go away. But in fact it didn't take me long to get ready. So, I needn't have got up so early. I could have stayed in bed longer. Men juda ir turdym, çünki gitmäne taýynlanmagyň zerurdy. Aslynda bolsa taýynlanmagyma köp wagt gitmedi, şonuň üçin beýle ir turmagym zerur däldi, ýerimde köpäk wagt ýatyp bilerdim.
