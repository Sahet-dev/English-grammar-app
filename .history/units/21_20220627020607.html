shall

A

I'll (I will) nähildir bir iş hereketi etmek üçin şol pursatda karar edilende ulanylýar:

Oh, I've left the door open. I'll go and shut it. - Gapyny açyk galdyrypdyryn. Gidip ony ýapjak.

"What would you like to drink?" "I’l have tea, please" "Näme içmek islärdiňiz?" "Men çaý içjek."

"Did you phone Ruth?" "Oh no, I forgot. 'l phone her now - "Ruta jaň etdiňmi? Ýok, unudypdyryn. Men oňa häzir jaň etjek."

Bu sözlemlerde present simple (I do /I go)-y ulanyp bolmaýar:

Oh, I’ve left the door open. I'll go and shut it. (I go and shut it" bolmaýar!)

"I think I'l..." we "I don't think I'll.." ýaly gurluşlarlar Hem köp ulanylýar:

I feel a bit hungry. I think I’ll have something to eat. Men biraz ajykýan ýaly. Pikirimçe birzatlar iýerin.

I don't think I’ll go out tonight. I'm too tired. Meniň pikirimçe men bu agşam daşaryk gitmerin. Men juda ýadow.

Iňlisçe sözleninede will-iň ýokluk formasy won’t(will not) aýdylýar:

I won't be late. Men gijikmerin.

B

Will birnäçe wagt ozal etmegä karar edilen iş-hereketi beýan etmek üçin ulanylmaýar.

I'm going on holiday next Saturday. – Geljek şenbe güni dynç alyşa çykýaryn. . ("I'll go" diýilmez)

C

Will köplenç aşakdaky mysallarda görkezlen pursatlarda ulanylýar.

That bag looks heavy. I'll help you with it. Sumka agyr görünýär. Men size kömek etjek.

Bir iş-hereketi etmäge razy bolnanda ulanylýar:

A: You know that book I lent you. Can I have it back if you've finished with it? – Saňa beren kitabymy bilýämiň. Eger ony tamamlan bolsaň ony gaýadyp alsam bolýarmy?
B:Of course. I’ll give it to you this afternoon. Elbetde. Men ony saňa günortan gaýdyp bererin.

Bir iş-hereket etmegi wada berlende:

Thanks for lending me the money. I'll pay you back on Friday. – Maňa karzyna pul bereniň üçin sagbol. Men ony saňa anna güni gaýtararyn. ('I pay' däl)

I won't tell anyone what happened. I promise. Näme bolanymy hiç kime aýtmaryn. Söz berýärin.

Kimdir birinden bir etmegi haýyş edilende:

Will you shut the door, please? – Gapyny ýaparsyňyz, haýyş?

Won't kimdir biri nähilidir bir iş-herekti etmegi ret edende:

I’ve tried to advise her but she won't listen. Oňa maslahat bermäge hereket etdim, ýöne ol gulak asmak islemedi. (diňlemegi ret etdi)

The car won't start. I wonder what's wrong with it. – Maşin işlemedi. Men haýran oňa näme boldy. . (Maşin işlemegi "ret etdi")

D

Shall ...? Shall we ...?

Shall esasan "Shall l/we...?" sorag sözlemlerde ulanylýar. Bu soraglar biriniň pikirini bilmek üçin, ýada köplenç tekliplerde ulanylýar:

Shall I open the window? - Aýnany açaýynmy?

I’ve got no money. What shall I do? - Pulum yok. Näme edýin?

Shall ...? we will you will? - y deňeşdiriň:

Shall I shut the door? - Gapyny ýapaýynmy?

Will you shut the door? - Gapyny ýapyrsyňyzmy?
