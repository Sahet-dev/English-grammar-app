How long
have you ...

A

Aşakdaky mysallar okaň:

Bob öýlenen. Ol mun 20 ýyl ozal öýlenipdi. Şeýlelik bilen onuň bugün toýunyň 20 ýyllygy.
He has been married for 20 years. Ol ýigrimi ýyldan bäri öýlenen.

Üns beriň:

He is married.
– Ol öýlenen. (simpfe present)

Emma

How long has he been married? / Ol haçandan bäri öýlenen? (present perfect)
(how long is he married" bolmaýar!)

He has been married for 20 years. – Ol 20 ýyldan bäri öýlenen. ("he is married for 20 years däl!)

Present perfect geçen zamanda başalanyp we henize, dowam edýän iş-herekti görkezmek üçin ulanylýar.
Present perfect'-i simple present we present continuous bilen deňeşdiriň:

We know each other very well.
- Biz biri-birimizi gowy bilýäris. emma
We have known each other for a long time.
Biz biri-birimizi köp wagtdan bäri bilýäris. ("we know" bolmaýar!)

Are you waiting for somebody?
– Sen birine garaşýaňmy? emma How long have you been waiting?-

I’ve been learning English for a long time.
Men iňlis dilini köpden bäri öwrenýärin. ("I am learning" däl)

Sorry I'm late. Have you been waiting long? / Bagyşlaň men gijikdim. Köpden bäri garaşýarmyň?

It's been raining since I got up this morning. / Men irden turanymdan bäri ýagyş ýagyp dur.

Iş-hereket gaýtalanyp durýan iş-hereket bolmagy hem mümkin: "How long have you been driving?" "Since I was 18"
- "Haçandan bäri maşyn sürýärsiň?" "18 ýaşymdan bäri"

C

I have done (simple) ýa-Duşanbede I have been doing (continuous).

Continuous adatça how long, since we for bilen bile ulanylýar.

I've been learning English for a long time. / Men iňlis dilini köp wagtdan bäri öwrenýärin ( "I've learnt" bolmaýar!)

Live we work işlikleri bilen Prezent continuous'-y hem, prezent simple'-i hem ulanmak bolýar:

John has been living / has lived in London for a long time.
- Jon Londonda köp wagt ýaşaýar.

How long have you been working/ have you worked here?
- Bu ýerde haçandan bäri ýaşaýarysyň?

Emma always bilen simple ulanylýar.

John has always lived in London. b-Jon hemişe Londonda ýaşan.("has always been living " däl!)

Köp wagt bäri gaýtalanyp gelýän iş-hereketi görkezmek üçin continuous ýa-da simple'-i ulanyp bolýar

I've been collecting/ l've collected stamps since I was a child. / Çagalygymdan bäri markalary ýygnap gelýärin.

Continuous'da adatça ulanylmaýan käbir işlikler bar mysal üçin: know/ like/ believe;

How long have you known Jane? / Jeýni haçandan bäri bilýärsiň? ("have you been knowing" bolup bilmez.)

D

Aşakdaky ýaly käbir ýokluk aňladýan sözlemlerde present perfect simple ulanylýar:

I haven't seen Tom since Monday.
- Duşanbeden bäri Tomy görmedim.

Jane hasn't phoned me for two weeks. / Jeýn maňa menga iki hepdeden bäri jaň etmedi.
