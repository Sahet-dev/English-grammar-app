A

Aşakdaky mysaly öwreniň:
Genri juda garry. Onuň ýaşy näçededigini hiç kim anyk bilmeýär.
Ýöne:
It is said that he is 108 years old.
Ýa-da He is said to be 108 years old.
Bu iki gürrüňiň manysy -(Adamlaryň) Aýtmaklarna görä onuň ýaşy 108 -de.


Bu gurluşyň birnäçe işlikler bilen ulanmak mümkin, esasanda:

thought believed considered reported known expected alleged understood

Bu iki gurluşy deňeşdiriň:
Cathy works very hard. – Keýsi köp işleýär.

It is said that she works 16 hours a day.

Ýa-da She is said to work 16 hours a day.

Terjimesi:Aýtmaklarna görä, ol gününe 16 sagat işleýär.

The strike started three weeks ago. Iştaşlama 3 hepde ozal başlady.

It is expected that it will end soon. Ýa-da The strike is expected to end soon.
Terjimesi: Iş taşalama ýakynda tiz ara gutarmagyna garaşylýar.

A friend of mine has been arrested. Dostlarymyň biri tussag edildi.

It is alleged that he kicked a policeman. Ýa-da He is alleged to have kicked a policeman.
Terjimesi: Ol polisiýanyň işgärini dependigi barada (esassyz) günäkärlenýär.
Adatça bu gurluş täze habarlar berlende (meselem heläkçilik barada) ulanylýar. It is reported that two people were injured in the explosion.

Ýa-da Two people are reported to have been injured in the explosion.
Terjimesi: Habar berilmegine görä, portlamada iki adam ýaralanan.

B

(Be) supposed to
It is supposed to.. –nyň manysy adatça it is said to..-nyňka deň.

Let's go and see that film. It's supposed to be very good. Hany gideliň we ol filmi göreris. Aýtmaklarna görä ol juda gowy. (= it is said to be very good)

"Why was he arrested?" "He's supposed to have kicked a policeman"- "ol näme üçin tussag edildi? Aýtmaklarna görä ol polisiýa işgäreine uran(depen) (= he is said to have kicked a policeman)

Ýöne käwagt supposed to-nyň manysy üýtgäp gelýär.

Something is supposed to happen - Bir zat bolmagyna garaşylýar.

Ýa-da meýillesdirlen. Köplenç bu aslynda bolup geçmeli zatdan tapawutly.
Türkmen dilinde köplenç ‘zerur, gerek’ sözlerini aňladýar.

I'd better hurry. It's nearly 8 o'clock and I'm supposed to be meeting Ann at 8.15. Howluganym makul. Sagat 8-e golaý, men bolsa sagat 8.15 -de En bilen duşuşmagym gerek. (Men En bilen duşuşmagy meýilleşdirdim. Oňa duşuşarys diýip aýdypdym)

The train was supposed to arrive at 11.30 but it was an hour late. – Otly 11.30-da gelmegi zerurdy, ýöne bir sagat gijikdi. (jedwele görä otlynyň gelmegi 11.30-a garaşylýardy)

You were supposed to clean the windows. Why didn't you do it? - Sen aýnalary arassalamagyň gerekdi. Näme üçin ony etmediň?

"You're not supposed to do something"- bir işi etmegiň zerurdy.

You're not supposed to park your car here. It's private parking only. – Sen bu ýere maşyny galdyrmazlygyň zerur. Bu hususy awtoulag duralga.

Mr. Bond is much better after his illness but he's still not supposed to do any heavy work. - Jenap Bond keselinden soň has gowy, ýöne henizem agyr iş etmeli däl.
