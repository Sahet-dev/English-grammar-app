When...? (+ past simple) we How long…? (+ present perfect)-i deňeşdiriň: A: When did it start raining? – Ýagyş haçan başlady?
B: It started raining an hour ago/ at 1 o'clock. - Ýagyş bir sagat ozal başlady/ sagat 1de başlady.

A: How long has it been raining? –Ýagyş haçandan bäri ýagyp dur?

B: It's been raining for an hour / since 1 o'clock. Ýagyş bir sagat bäri ýagyp dur / sagat 1den bäri ýagyp dur.

A: When did Joe and Carol first meet?- Jo we Kerol birinji gezek haçan duşuşdylar?

B: They first met a long time ago/ when they were at school. – Olaryň birinji duşuşygy köp wagt mundan ozal/ (olar mekdepdekäler) bolupdy.

A: How long have Joe and Carol known each other?-Jo we Kerol biri- birini haçandan bäri bilýärler?

B: They've known each other. Olar biri-birlerini bilýärler.

It's two years since I last saw Joe. – Jony soňky gezek görenime iki ýyl boldy.

It's ages since we went to the cinema. Kino soňky gezek baranymyz bäri asyrlar geçdi.
