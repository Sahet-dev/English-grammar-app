should (2)

A

Should adatça aşakdaky işlikler bilen köp ulanylýar

suggest
propose
recommend
  insist
  demand

I demanded that he should apologise. - Men Onuň ötünç soramagyny talap etdim.

What do you suggest I should do? – Näme etmegimi maslahat berýärsiňiz?

They insisted that we should have dinner with them. – Olar bilen nahar iýmelidigimizi gerekdigini aýtdylar.

Şu görnüşde should, suggestion/proposal/ recommendation şuňa meňzeşlerden hem soň getirilip ulanylýar.

What do you think of Jane's suggestion that should buy a car? – Jeýniň maňa maşyn almagym gerekdigi hakyndaky teklibi barada pikiriňiz nähili?

Should şeýlede "it's important/vital/ necessary/essential that..." -lardan soň ulanylýar:
It's essential that you should be here on time. Bu ýerde öz wagtynda bolmagyňyz juda möhimdir.

B

A bölümdedäki mysallaryň hemmesinde should –y galdyryp ulanyp bolýar:
I demanded that he apologise. ( that he should apologise) - Men onuň soramagyny talap etdim.

What do you suggest I do?- Maňa näme etmegimi maslahat berýärsiňiz?

Bu forma (I do / he apologise) käwagt subjunctive diýip aýdylýar.

Şonuň bilen bile present we past zamanlaryny hem ulanmak mümkin:
It's essential that you are here on time. Bu Ýerde öz wagtynda bolamgyň gaty zerurdyr.

I demanded that he apologised. - Men onuň ötünç soramagyny talap etdim.

Suggest-e üns beriň, ondan soň "to" (to do/to buy we ş.m) ulanmak mümkin däl.

Jane suggested that I (should) buy a car. Jeýn maşyn satyn almagymy maslahat berdi.

Ýa-da Jane suggested that I bought a car. (emma "Jane suggested me to buy a car" däl)

Suggest +ing -ni Unit 52-de göriň.

C

Should birnäçe sypatlar bbilen bile ulanylyşy:

strange
odd
funny
typical
natural
interesting
surprised
surprising

It's strange that he should be late. He's usually on time. – Onuň giç galýany geň. Ol adatça öz wagtynda gelýär.

I was surprised that she should say such a thing. Onuň beýle gürlänine haýran galdym.

D

If.. should..

"If something should happen"- " egerde bir zat bolan ýagdaýda."

If Tom should phone when I'm out, tell him I'll phone him back later. Egerde men daşary çykanymda Tom jaň etse oňa men yzynajaň etjekdigimi aýdaýyň.

"If Tom should phone"-nyň manysy "If Tom Phones"nyňka meňzeýär.
Haçanda Should ulanylanda sözleýji iş herekteiň bolmak ähtimallygynyň pesdigini bildirýär.
Ýenede mysal:
I've left the washing outside. If it should rain, can you bring it in? – Kirlerni daşarda galdyrypdym. Eger ýagyş ýagsa sen ony içeri getirip bilersiňmi?

Should –y bu sözlemiň başynda hem ulanmak ýalňyş bolmaz:
Should Tom phone...- Eger Tom jaň etse, ...

E

I should.. /I shouldn't... birine maslahat berilýän pursatda hem ulanmak mümkin:
"Shall I leave?" "No, I should wait a bit longer"-"Men gidibereýinmi?" "Yok, men ýene biraz garaşardym."

Bu ýerde "I should wait" "seniň ornuňda bolanymda men garaşardym, saňa garaşmagy maslahat berýärin.” Diýen manyny berýär.
