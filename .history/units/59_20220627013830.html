predlog
(in, for, about) + -ing ...

A

Predlogdan (in/for/about we ş.m) soň işlik gelse şol işlige –ing goşulýar.

Mysal üçin:
predlog + (ing)


Are you interested in working for us? Biz üçin işlemäge gyzyklanýarsyňmy?

I'm not very good at learning languages. Men dil öwrenmek ukybym juda gowy däl.

She must be fed up with studying. Ol okamakdan bizar bolandyr.

This knife is only for cutting bread. Bu pyçak diňe çöregi kesmek üçin niýetlenen.

How about playing tennis? Tenis oýnasak nähili?

Carol went to work in spite of being il. Keral kesel bolsada işe gitdi.

Let's go home instead of going to the party. Oturlyşygyň ýerne ýöriň öýe gideliň.

"Interested in somebody (do)ing..." "fed up with you (doing... we şular ýaly sözlemler hem bar:

I'm fed up with you telling me what to do. Näme etmegimi aýtmagyňdan bizar.

B

Aşakdaky prodlogdan soň -ing ulanylýandygyny ýatda saklaň.

before -ing we after –ing:
Before going out, I phoned Sarah. Daşary çykmazdan ozal men Sara jaň etdim.

What did you do after leaving school?- Mekdebi tamamlandan soň näme etdiňiz?

Ýokardaky mysallarda "Before I went out..." we after you left school" diýmek hem mumkin.

by -ing (bir zadyň nähili ýagdaýda bolýanyny beýan etmek üçin):
The burglars got into the house by breaking a window. Ogrylar öýe aýnany döwmek arkaly girdiler.

You can improve your English by reading more. – Iňlis dilini köpräk okap gowlandyryp bilersiň.

She made herself ill by not eating properly. Ol dogry naharlanman hassa boldy.

without -ing:

I ran ten kilometres without stopping. – Men durman on kilometr ylgadym.

They climbed through the window without anybody seeing them (ýa-da without being seen).- Olar hiç kim görmänkä aýnadan aşdylar.

She needs to work without people disturbing her (ýa-da without being disturbed). – Ol hiç kimiň päsgel bermesiz işlemegi zerur.

It's nice to go on holiday without having to worry about money. - Pul barada oýlanman dynç alyşa gitmek gowy.

C

To -ing

To adatça infinitiwiň başdaky bölegi (to see / to do we ş.m)

We decided to go out. - Biz köçä çykmana karar etdik.

Would you like to play tennis?- Tenis oýnamany işlärdiňizmi?

Emma to predlog hem bolýandyr (in/for/about/from we şuňa meňzeşler ýaly).
Mysal üçin:
We drove from London to Edinburgh. – Biz maşyny Londondan Edinburga sürdik.

I prefer tea to coffee. Men çaýy kofeden makul görýärin.

Eger predlog soň işlik gelse şol işlik –ing bilen tamamlanýar. (in doing/about going we ş.m). Diýmek to predlog bolup ondan soň işlik gelen ýagdaýda to -ing goşulyp ulanylýar:

I prefer driving to travelling by train. – Otlyda syýahat edenimden maşynda gezmegi artykrak görýärin.

Are you looking forward to seeing Ann again? Eni ýene görmäne sabyrsyzlyk bilen garaşýarsyňmy?
