A

Aşakdaky mysallary öwreniň:
A: Will you phone me tomorrow? - Ertir maňa jaň edersiňmi? B: Yes, I'll phone you when I get home from work. Hawa, men saňa işden öýe gelenimde jaň ederin.

"I’Il phone you when l get home from work". iki bölümden ybarat bolan gürrüň.
esasy bölümi: I’ll phone you
we when bölümi: "when I get home from work (tomorrow)"

"I’ll phone you" Sözlemiň zamany geljek zaman (tomorrow ertir), ýöne sözlemiň when-bölüminde present simple (get) ulanyldy.

Sözlemiň when bölüminde will ulanmak mümkin däl:

We'll go out when it stops raining. Köçä haçanda ýagyş dynanda çykarys. ("when it will stop" bolmaýar)

When you are in London again, you must come and see us. – Siz Londonda ýene bolanyňyzda bizi hökman görip gitmelisiňiz. (when you will be" däl)

(ýaş oglanjyga) What do you want to be when you grow up? - Sen ulalaňda kim bolmak isleýärsiň? ("will grow" däl)

Ýokardaky düzgün aşakdaky baglaýjylara hem degişli:
while before after as soon as until/till

I'm going to read a lot of books while l'm on holiday. –Haçanda men dynç alyşda bolanymda köp kitap okamakçy.

Wait here until (ýa-da till) I come back. Men yzyma dolanýançam şu taýda garaş.

B

When/ after/ until/ as soon as -lardan soňra present perfect- hem ulanmak mümkin:

Can I borrow that book when you've finished it?- Sen kitaby okap gutaranda soňra ony okamaga alsam bolarmy?

Don't say anything while Ian is here. Wait until he has gone. Aýen bärde barka hiç zat diýmäň. Onuň gitmegine garaş.

Köplenç present simple-i hem, present Perfect-i ham ulanyp bolýar:

I'll come as soon as I finish ýa-da l'll come as soon as I've finished – Men işimi gutardygym gelerin.

Ýöne iki waka wagtda ýa-da bile bolup geçende present perfect ulanylmaýar. Present perfect bir wakanyň ikinji wakadan ozal tamamlanýandygyny görkezýär.
(diýmek olar bir wagtda bolup geçmeýär).

Dňeşdiriň:
When I've phoned Kate, we can have dinner. Keýte jaň edenimden soňra nahar iýip bileris. (Ozal Keýte jaň ederin, ondan soňra nahar iýeris)

C

Geljek zamany beýan etmek üçin adatça present simple ulanylýar. (if I do / if l see)

Hurry up! If we don't hurry, we'Il be late. Howlukmaly ! Eger howlukmasak biz gijä galarys.

When we if-i deňeşdiriň:
When bolup geçmegi takyk bolan hadysalar üçin ulanylýar:

I'm going shopping this afternoon. When I go shopping, I'll buy some food. – Men günortan söwda etmäge giderin.(takyk bolmaly hadysa) Söwda etmäge gidenimde iýmit hem alaryn.

If (when däl) bolup geçmegi mümkinbolan hadysalar üçin ulanylýar:

I might go shopping this afternoon. If I go shopping, I'll buy some food. Men günortan söwda etmäge gitmagim ahmal.(belki...) Eger söwda etmäge gitsem iýmit hem alaryn.

If it is raining this evening, I won't go out. – Eger agşam ýagyş ýagsa men daşary aýlanmana çykmaryn.
