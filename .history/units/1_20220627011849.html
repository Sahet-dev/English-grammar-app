DOING

A

Aşakdaky mysaly okaň we derňewini öwreniň:

Anna awtoulagda otyr. Ol ishe baryar.
She is driving to work. - Ol ishe mashynly gidip barýar.

Anna edil şu pursatda yagny sözleşilýän pursatda maşynly gidip barýar. Iş hereket entäk tamamlanmady. Am/is/are -ing -present continuous'-yň yasalyş formasy: I am driving./ I'm driving.
He is/ she is working. / He;s/ she's working.
We are / you are/ they are doing.
We’re/ you’re/ they're doing.

B

Present continuous tense
Sözlem sözlenip durlan pursatda iş hereketiň dowam edip duranyny şekillendirmek üçin ulanylýar.

I am doing something = Men bir zatlar edýärin. Ýagny, men ony edip durun, Men bir iş herekti başlap ony entäk tamamlamadym.

Adatça iş hereket sözleşilýän pursatda bolup geçýär.

Please, don't make so much noise. I am working. Haýyş beyle gaty galmagal etmäň, men işleýärin. (“l work” Ulanylmaz)
