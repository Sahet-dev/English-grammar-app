ver + (dolduryjy) + to ...
i want (you) to do...

A

want
ask
help
expect

would like
mean (bir iş etmegi niýet etmek)
would love,
would hate,
would prefer Aşakdaky işliklerden soň to... ulanylýar.
Gurluş şeýle görnüşde bolmagy mümkin:

Işlilk + to.. ýa-da işlik + dolduryjy + to..

We expected to be late. – Biz gijikgeris diýip garaşypdyk.

We expected Tom to be late. Biz Tom gijiger diýip garaşypdyk.

Would you like to go now? Häzir gitmegi islärdiňmi?

Would you like me to go now? – Meniň häzir gitmegimi islärdiňmi?

He doesnt want to know. – Ol bilmegi islemeýär.

He doesn't want anybody to know. – Ol hiç kimiň bilmegini islemeýär.

Want işligine üns beriň. "Want that.." gurluş ulanylmaýar:
Do you want me to come with you? – Meniň siz bilen barmagymy isleýärsiňizmi? ("Do you want that I come" däl)

Help işliginden soň infinitiwi to bilen hem, to-syz hem ulanmak mümkin:
Can you help me to move this table? Ýa-da Can you help me move this table? Bu stoly süýşürmäne maňa kömek edip bilersiňmi?

tell

Aşakdaky işlikler işlik+ dolduryjy+ to... gurluşy bilen ulanylýar.

Who taught you to drive? - Kim saňa sürmäni öwretdi?

I didn't move the piano by myself. I got somebody to help me. - Pianinony özüm süýşürmedim. Maňa kömek etmäne birini tapdym.

Jim said the switch was dangerous and warned me not to touch it. – Jim wiklýuçateliň howply ekenini aýt we ony ellemezligimi duýdurdy.

Indiki mysalda işlik passive görnüşde (was warned):
I was warned not to touch the switch. – Men wiklýuçateli ellemezligim barada duýduryldym

Işlik + dolduryjy + to... gurluşy bilen suggest-iň ulanylmaýandygyny ýatda saklaň:

Jane suggested that I should buy a car. - Jeyn maňa maşyn almagym gerekdigi barada maslahat berdi.

C

advise

Ýokardaky işlikleri iki hili gurluşda ulanmak mümkin. deňeşdiriň:

işlik +-ing (dolduryjysyz) we iş;il + dolduryjy + to.

She doesn't allow smoking in the house. – Ol gyz öýde çilim çekmäne rugsat bermeýär.

She doesn't allow us to smoke in the house. Ol gyz bize öýde çilim çekmäne rugsat bermeýär.

Bu mysaly işligiň passive formasy (be allowed) bilen deňeşdiriň:
Smoking isn't allowed in the house. Öýde çilim çekmäne rugsat berilmeýär.

We are not allowed to smoke in the house.- Bize öýde çilim çekmäne rugsat bermeýär.

D

Makeb web let

Bu iki işlik işlik + dolduryjy + infinitive (to-syz) gurluş bilen ulanylýar:
The customs officer made Sally open her case. – Gümrük ofiseri Sälliniň çemodanyn açdyrdy.

Hot weather makes me feel tired. Yssy howa meni ýadow duýdurtýar.

Her parents don't let her go out alone. -Onuň ata-enesi oňa köçä ýalňyz çykmana rugsat bermeýär.

"Make somebody do..."gurluşy passive-de"(be) made to do" (to bilen) görnüşde bolýar:
Sally was made to open her case. Sällä çemodanyny açdyrdylar.
