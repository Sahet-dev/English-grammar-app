question
forms

A

Adatça soraglar söz tertibini üýtgedip düzülýär:
Tom will / Will Tom? Will Tom be here tomorrow? Ertir Tom şu ýerde bolaýarmy?

you have /have you?

Have you been working hard? - Sen köp işleýärsiňmi?

I can /can I?

What can I do? – Näme edip bilersiň? ("What I can do?" däl)

the house was / was the house?

When was the house built?- Öý haçan gurlan.

B

Present simple-daky soraglar do/does ulanylýar:

you live /do you live?
Do you live near here?- Sen şu golaýda ýaşaýarsyňmy?

the film begins / does the film begin?

What time does the film begin? – Kino näwagt başlaýar?

Past simple-daky soraglarda did ulanylýar:
you sold/ did you sell?

Did you sell your car? – Sen maşynyňy satdyňmy?

the shop opened / did the shop open?

When did the shop open? Dükan haçan açyldy?

Emma sözlemde eýe bolup who/what/which -ler gelse, do/does/did ulanylmaýar.

Dňeşdiriň:
who - dolduryjy

Emma telephoned somebody.

Who did Emma telephone? Emma kime jaň etdi?


who -eýe

Somebody telephoned Emma.

Who telephoned Emma? Kim Emma-a jaň etdi?

Aşakdaky mysallarda who/what/which sözler hökmünde gelýär:
Who wants something to eat?- Kim bir zatlar iýmek isleýär? ("Who does want?" däl)

What happened to you last night? – Saňa düýn näme boldy? ("What did happen?" däl)

Which bus goes to the city centre?- Şäher merkezine haýsy awtobuz gidýär?

C

Who/what/which/where.. bilen başlanýan soraglarda predloglaryň ulanylyşyna üns beriň.

Who do you want to speak to? Kim bilen gürleşmek isleýäň?

Which job has Jane applied for? -Jeýn haýsy işe dalaşgär boldy(ýazyldy)?

Where do you come from? -Nirelisiň? (Sen nireli?)

What was the weather like yesterday? Düýn howa nähilidi?

D

Negatiw (ýoklyk) soraglar (isn't it.? / didn't you..?)

Ýoklyk soraglar esasan haýran galmagy görkezmek üçin ýa-da diňleýjiniň size goşulmagyna garaşylanda ulanylýar:

Didn't you hear the bell? I rang it four times. -Jaňy aşitmediňmi? Men ony dört gezek çaldym.

"Haven't we met somewhere before?" "Yes, I think we have." - "Biz ozal bir ýerde görüşmänmidik?" "Hawa, meniň pikirimçe görüşdik."

Isn't it a beautiful day!- Ajaýyp gün! (= It's a beautiful day, isn't it? – Gün ajaýyp, hämi?)

Negatiw soraglaryň jogaplaryndaky yes we no-nyň manylarna üns beriň:
Don't you want to sleep? – Uklamakçy dälmiň?
Yes. - Hawa(= Hawa, uklamakçy).
No.- Ýok (=Ýok, uklamakçy däl).

Why bilen başlanýan soraglardaky söz tertibine üns beriň:

Why don't we go out for a meal tonight? - Bugün Näme üçin bu agşam nahara çykmaýarys?

Why wasn't Mary at work yesterday? Düýn näme üçin Meri işde däldi? ("Why Mary wasn't..." däl)
