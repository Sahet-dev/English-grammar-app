A

Aşakdaky mysallary öwreniň:
Jiliň öýüniň üçegine tupan zeper ýetiripdi, şonuň üçin Jil ony bejertmek üçin ussa ýüz tutdy. Düýn ussa gelip işi bitirdi.
Jill had the roof repaired yesterday. – Düýn Jil Uçegini bejertdi.

Munuň manysy: Jil üçegi abatlamak üçin birine ýüz tutdy. Ony özi abatlamady.

Deňeşdiriň:
Jill repaired the roof. - Jil üçegini abatlady. (özi)

Jill had the roof repaired. Jil üçegini abatlatdy. (bir kişä)

Aşakdaky gürrüňleri öwreniň:
Did Ann make the dress herself or did she have it made? Köýnegi Eniň özi tikdimi ýa ol ony birine etdirdimi?

"Are you going to repair the car yourself?" "No, I'm going to have it repaired." "Maşyny özüň bejermekçimiň?" "Yok, men ony bejertmekçi

Sözler tertibine üns beriň. Geçen zaman sypatdaşy (past participle) (repaired/cut we ş.m) dolduryjydan (object) (the roof / your hair we ş.m) soň gelýär:
Jill had the roof repair yesterday.
Where did you have your hair cut.
Julia has just had central heating installed in her hous.
I do not like having my photograph taken.
We are having the house painted at the moment .
How often do you have serviced.

B

Şeýlede have something done-nyň ýerine get something done gurluşy ulanmak mümkin( esasan resmi däl sözleýişde):

When are you going to get the roof repaired? Haçan üçegi abatlatmakçy? (= have the roof repaired)

I think you should get your hair cut. Meniň pikirimçe sen saçyňy almaly.

C

Käwagt have something done başga manyda gelýär.
Mysal üçin:
Jill and Eric had all their money stolen while they were on holiday. - Jill we Erik dynç alyşda ähli pullaryny ogurlatdylar. Elbetde bu sözlemde olaryň pullaryny ogurlamak üçin başga birini hakyna tutandyklaryny aňlatmaýar.

Munuň manysy - "olaryň hemme pullary olardan ogurlandy."

Bu many arklay "have something done" birine ýa-da özüne degişli nähilidir bir zada nämedir bolandygyny aňladýar. Adatça bolup geçýän zatlar lapykeçlikdir.

George had his nose broken in a fight. Tutluşykda Jorjyň burny döwüldi. Have you ever had your passport stolen? - Pasportyňyzy ogurladyp gördüňizmi?
