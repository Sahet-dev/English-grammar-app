A

Aşakdaky mysaly öwreniň:
This house was built in 1935. Bu öý 1935 ýylda gurlan.

"Was built" - işligiň passiw paýy.

Işligiň anyk (active) passive paýlaryny deňeşdiriň:
Somebody built his house in 1935.
This house was built in 1935. Bu öý 1935 yilda gurlan. (passive)

Işligiň anyk paýy (active voice) eýe tarapyndan ýerine ýetirlen iş-hereketi görkezmek üçin ulanylýar:
My grandfather was a builder. He built this house in 1935. Meniň atam ussady. Ol bu öýi 1935-nji ýylda guran.

It's a big company. It employs two hundred people. Bu uly kompaniýa. Ol iki ýüz kişini iş bilen üpjün edýär.

Işligiň passiw gatnaşygy, eýesiniň üstünde edilmeli hereketi görkezmek üçin ulanylýar:
This house is quite old. It was built in 1930. - Bu öý gaty köne. Ol 1930-njy ýylda gurlan.

Two hundred people are employed by the company. - Bu kompaniýada iki ýüz kşi iş bilen üpjün edilen.

B

Passive ulanylanda iş-hereketiň kim tarapyndan ýerine ýetirlendigi nämälim ýa-da möhüm bolmaýar:
A lot of money was stolen in the robbery. Talaňçylykdda örän köp pul ogurlandy.(kimdir ogurlady, ýöne kim ogurlany nämälim)

Is this room cleaned every day?- Bu otag hergüni arassalanýarmy? (kimdir biri otagy arassalaýany möhüm däl)

Iş-hereketiň kim tarapyndan ýerine ýetirilýändigini görkezmek üçin by (tarapyndan) ulanylýar:
This house was built by my grandfather. – Bu öý meniň atam tarapyndan gurlan. (öýi atam guran)
Two hundred people are employed by the company. – Kompaniýa tarapyndan iki ýüz kişi iş bilen üpjün edilen.
