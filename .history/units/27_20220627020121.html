Could (do)
could have (done)

A

Could –y her dürli edip ulanyp bolýar. Adatça could Can-iň geçen zaman hökmünde bolýar:

I can swim. - Men suwda ýüzüp bilýärin. (häzir)

I could swim when I was a child. Haçanda men ýaş wagtym suwda ýüzüp bilýärdim

Emma could diňe bir şu düşünjäni aňlatmak üçin ulnylmaýar. Could häzir ýa-da geljekde bolup geçjemegi mümki bolan hadysalar hakynda gürrüň edilende hem ulanylyp bilner.(esasan teklip edilende)
Mysal üçin:
What shall we do this evening?

We could go to the cinema

A: What shall we do this evening? Agşam näme ederis?

B: We could go to the cinema. Biz kino gidip bileris.

It's a nice day. We could go for a walk. – Gün ajajýyp. Gezelenç etmäge gidip bilerdik.

When you go to New York next month, you could stay with Barbara. –Geljek aýda Nýu Ýorka gideniňde Barbaranyň öýünde galyp bilersiň.

Ýokardaky sözlemlerde can-y hem ulanyp bolýar ("we can go for a walk" we ş.m).
Could bilen can-i deňeşdirlende gowşagrak bolany ynamy beýan edýär. Aýdylan gürrüň aslynda amala aşyrylmasa Could -y (can -i däl) ulanmak zerur. Mysal üçin:
I'm so hungry. I could eat a bull. – Men şeýle bir aç. Hatda öküzi iýip blerin.

B

Şeýlede could bir wakanyň häzir ýa-da geljekde bolmagy mümkindigini görkezmek üçin ulanylýar:

The phone is ringing. It could be Tim. - Telefon jyňyrdaýar. Bu Tim bolmagy mümkin.

I don't know when they'll be here. They could arrive at any time. – Men olaryň nä wagt bärde boljagyny bilemak. Olar islendik wagt gelmekleri mümkin.

Şular ýaly gürrüňlerde can -i ulanyp bolmaýar. ("It can be Tim" däl).

Could bu gürrüňlerde might –a meňzeýär.

The phone is ringing. It might be Tim.- Telefon jyňyrdaýar. Bu Tim bolmagy mümkin.

C

Could (do) we could have (done)-ny deňeşdiriň:

I'm so tired. I could sleep for a week. – Men juda ýadow. Bir hepde uklap bilerin. (häzir)

I was so tired. I could have slept for a week. Men juda ýadowdym. Bir hepde uklap bilmedim. (geçmişde)

Köplenç could have (done) bolmagy ähtimal bolan, ýöne bolmadyk wakalary görkezmek üçin ulanylýar:

Why did you stay at a hotel when you went to New York? You could have stayed with Barbara. Sen näme üçin Nýu Ýorkda bolanyňda myhmanhanada ýaşadyň. Sen Barbaranyň öýünde galyp bilerdiňä. (sende şeýle mümkinçilik bardy, ýöne sen ettmediň)

The situation was bad but it could have been worse. – Ýagdaý agyrdy emma mundan ýamanrak bolmagy hem mümkindi.

D

Could käwagt "would be able to" (edip bilerdi) diýen manyny bildirýär:

We could go away if we had enough money. Biziň pulumyz ýeterlik bolanda biz gidip bilerdik.

I don't understand how you work so hard. I couldn't do it. – Seniň beýle köp işleýäniňe düşünemak. Men beýle edip bilmerin.

Could have (done)-"would have been able to do" (edip başaran bolardy).

We could have gone away if we'd had enough money. - Eger biziň pulymyz ýeterlik boland giden (başaran) bolardyk.

The trip was cancelled last week. Paul couldn't have gone anyway because he was ill. - Geçen hepde syýahat galdyryldy. Pol her niçigem bolsa (barybir) gidip bilmezdi, sebäbi ol hassady.

You did very well to pass the exam. I'm sure couldn’t have passed it. – Ekzaminden geçeniň örän gowy iş boldy. Men ynanýaryn, men ondan geçmegi başarmazdym.
