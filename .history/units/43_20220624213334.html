Passive(3)

A

I was born..

Adatça şu formada ulanylýar: I was born.. ("I am born.." däl)- Men... doguldym.

I was born in Chicago. - Men Çikago-da doguldym. past simple

Where were you born? Sen nirde doguldyň? ("where are you born" däl) past simple

Emma How many babies are born every day? Her gün näçe çaga dogulýar? present simple

B

Käbir işlikler bilen iki dolduryjy (object) ulanmak mümkin
Mysal üçin give işligi:
We gave the police the information. Biz polisiýa maglumay berdik.
Diýmek, bu gürrüňden iki passive gürrüň ýasamak mümkin:
The police were given the information. - Polisiýa maglumat berildi. (Polisiýa maglumat üpjün edildi.)

Ýa-da The information was given to the police. Maglumat polisiýa berildi.

işlikler bilen hem iki dolduryjy (object) gelmegi mümkin: ask offer pay show teach tell

Bu işlikler passive –de ulanylanda gürrüň adatça şahs bilen başlanýar:
I was offered the job but I refused it. – Maňa iş teklip edildi emma men ony ret etdim.

You will be given plenty of time to decide. Karar kabul etmek üçin saňa köp wagt beriler.

Have you been shown the new machine? Saňa täze maşyny görkezdilermi.

C

I don't like being...
Doing/seeing we şuňa meňzeşleriň passive şekili being done / being seen we ş.m.
Deňeşdiriň:
active:I don't like people telling me what to do. - Maňa näme etmelidigimi aýtýan adamlary halamaýaryn.

passive:I don't like being told what to do. Maňa näme etmelidigimi aýdylmasyny halamaýaryn.

I remember being given a toy drum on my fifth birthday. – Bäş ýaşymda oýunjak deprek sowgat edenleri ýadymda.

Mr. Miller hates being kept waiting. – Jenap Miller garaşdyryp goýulmagyny ýigrenýär.
We managed to climb over the wall without being seen. – Diwardan hiç kime görünmän geçmegi başardyk.

D

Get

Käte passive de be –niň ornuna get-i ulanmak mümkin.

There was a fight at the party but nobody got hurt. Şagalaňda uruş boldy emma hiç kim şikeslenmedi.(= nobody was hurt)

I don't often get invited to parties. Oturlyşyklara ýygy-ýygydan çagyrylmaýaryn. (= I'm not often invited)

Get sözlemdäki eýä bir hadysa bolup geçende, esasanam şol hadysa garaşylmadyk pursatda bolanda ulanylýar.

My finger got cut while cooking.-Nahar taýarlaýarkam barmagym kesildi.

Get diňe birine bir hadysa bolanda ýa-da bir pursatda üýtgeşme bolanda ulanylýar. Mysal üçin aşakdaky gürrüňlerde get-i ulanmak mümkin däl:

Jill is liked by everybody. Jil hemmä ýarady. ("gets liked" däl - bu bir waka däl)

He was a mystery man. Nothing was known about him. – Ol syrly adamdy. Ol hakda hiçkim bilmeýärdi. (got known – däl )

Get esasan resmi däl sözleýişde ulanylýar, be-ni bolsa hemme pursatda ulanmak mümkin.

Şeýlede get aşakdaky görnüşde ulanylýar. (hatda olaryň manysy passive-de bolmasada):
