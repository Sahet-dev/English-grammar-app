A

Işligiň aşakdaky active we passive formalaryny öwreniň:
Infinitive
active (to) do/clean
passive (to) be done/cleaned
Somebody will clean the room later. Biri soňrak otagy arassalar.

The room will be cleaned later. – Otag soňrak arassalanar.

The situation is serious. Something must be done before it's too late. – Ýagdaý agyr. Giç bolmanka bir zat etmegimiz zerur.

The music could be heard from a long way away. Aýdym uzakdan eşidilýärdi.

A new supermarket is going to be built next year. Täze supermarket geljek ýyl gurulmakçy.

Please go away. I want to be left alone. Haýyşt gidiň. Men ýalňyz galmak isleýärin.

B

Perfect infinitive
active have done/cleaned
passive have been done/cleaned

Somebody should have cleaned the room. Kimdir biri otagy arassalamalydy.

The room should have been cleaned. - Otag arassalanmalydy

I haven't received the letter yet. It might have been sent to the wrong address. – Haty heniz kabul etmedim. Ol nädogry adresa (salga) berlen bolmagy ahmal.

If you hadn't left the car unlocked, it wouldn't have been stolen. – Maşyny açyk galdyrmadyk bolanyňda ol ogurlanan bolmazdy.

C

Present perfect
active have/has (done)
passive have/has been (done)

Somebody has cleaned the room. – Otagy kimdir arassalan.
The room has been cleaned. Otag arassalanan.

Have you heard the news? The criminal has been caught! Täzeligi eşitdiňmi? Jenaýatçy saklandy!

"Are you going to the party?" "No, I haven't been invited." Oturluşyga barýarsyňm? Yok. Men teklip edilmedim.

Past perfect
active had (done)
passive had been (done)
Somebody had cleaned the room. Otagy kimdir biri arassalapdy.

The room had been cleaned. Otag arassalanypdy.

The vegetables didn't taste very good. They had been cooked for too long. Gök önümler gaty tagamly däldi. Olar gaty uzak wagtlap bişirilipdi.

The car was three years old but hadn't been used very much. - Maşyna üç ýyl boldy, ýöne kän ulanylmady.

Present continuous
active am/is/are (do)ing
passive am/is/are being (done)

Somebody is cleaning the room now. – Häzir kimdir biri otagy arassalaýar.

The room is being cleaned now. – Otag häzir arassalanýar.

Can I use your pen, please? It's being used now. - Men galamyňyzy ulanyp bilerinmi? Häzir ol ulanylýar.

Past continuous
active was/were (do)ing
passive was/were being (done)
Somebody was cleaning the room when I arrived. Haçanda men gelenimde kimdir biri otagy arassalaýardy.

The room was being cleaned when l arrived.- Haçanda men gelenimde otagy arassalanýardy.

I couldn't use the telephone because it was being used. – Men telefondan ulanyp bilmedim, çünki ol ulanylýardy.
