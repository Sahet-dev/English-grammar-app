would
i wish ...would

A

Would ('d) bir wakany ýa-da iş hereketi göz öňüne getirlende ulanylýar:
It would be nice to buy a new car, but we can't afford it. – Täze amşyn satyn alsak gaty gowy bolardy, emma oňa gurbumyz ýetmeýär.

Would have (done) bir pursady ýa-da iş-hereketi geçen zamanda göz öňüne getirlende ulanylýar:
They helped me a lot. I don't know what I would have done without their help. - Olar maňa köp kömek etdiler. Olaryň kömegisiz men näme edip biljegimi bilmeýärin

B

Will (‘ll) we would ('d)-lary deňeşdiriň:

I'll stay a bit longer. I’ve got plenty of time. - Men ýene birazajyk galjak. Wagtym köp

I'd stay a bit longer but I really have to go now.- Men ýene birazajyk galardym, emma häzir çyndanam gitmegim zerur. (diýmek men mundan beýläk galyp bilmeýärin)

Would/wouldn't adatça will/won't-yň geçen formasy. Deňeşdiriň:
present
TOM: I'll phone on Sunday. – Ýekşenbe güni jaň etjek.
past
Tom said he'd phone on Sunday. Tom ýekşenbe güni jaň etjekdigini aýtdy.
present
ANN:I promise I won't be late. Gijikmezlige wada berýärin.
past
Ann promised she wouldn't be late. En gijikmezlige wada berdi.

C

I wish... would..
Aşakdaky mysaly öwreniň:
Ýagyş ýagyp dur. Jil köçä çykmakçy ýöne emma ýagyşda däl.

Jill: I wish it would stop raining. – Ýaggyş dynmagyny islärdim.
Manysy: Jil ýagyşdan şikaýat edýär, ol ýagyş dynmagyny isleýär. I wish... would... bir zadyň bolmagyny ýa-da kimdir biriniň bir zady etmegini islände ulnaylýar. Munda sözleýjini häzirki pursatda närazy.

The phone has been ringing for five minutes. I wish somebody would answer it. – Telefon bäş minutdan bäri jyňyrdap dur. Oňa kimdir biri jogap bermegini islärdim.

I wish you would do something instead of just sitting and doing nothing. – Men seniň ýöne oturanyňdan we hiç zat etmeýäniň ýerine bir zatlar etmegiňi islärdim.

I wish... would... adamlaryň gaýta-gaýta etýän işlerine närazylyk bildirlende ulanmak mümkin:
I wish you wouldn't keep interrupting me. Meniň gepimi bölmezligiňi isleýän.

I wish.. would... pursatlar üçin däl, eýsem iş-hereket we özgerişler üçin ulanylýar:
I wish Sarah would come. Saranyň gelmegini islärdim.

Emma I wish Sarah were/ was here now. Saranyň häzir şu ýerde bolimagyny is lärdim. ("I wish Sarah would be..." däl)

D

Şeýlede would geçmişdde yzgiderli gaýtalnyp duran işler barada gürrüň edilende ulanylýar:
When we were children, we lived by the sea. In summer, if the weather was fine, we would all get up early and go for a swim. Çagalygymyzda biz deňiziň gyrasynda ýaşaýardyk . Ýazda howa gowy bolan günleri hemmämiz ir turup deňize ýüzmäne giýärdik. (muny yzgiderli adýärdik)
