it's no use...,
it's no good...

A

Aşakdakylar ýaly söz birikmelerden soň işlik gelen ýagdagda şol işlige –ing goşulýar.

It's no use... / It's no good..

There's nothing you can do about the situation, so it's no use worrying about it. Bu ýagdaýda hiç zat edip bolmaýar, şonuň üçin ony alada etmegiň geregi ýok. .

It's no good trying to persuade me. You won't succeed. - Meni yrmana synanyşygyňyz gowy däl. Siz meni yryp bilmeersiňiz.

Theres no point in...:

There's no point in having a car if you never use it. Maşyny hiçem ulanmasaň ony satyn alanyň peýdasy ýok.

It's (not) worth..

I live only a short walk from here, so it's not worth taking a taxi. Men men şu golaýda ýaşaýaryn , şonuň üçin taksi tutmagyň geregi ýok.

"A film is worth seing", "a book is worth reading" ýaly söz düzülmeleri hem ulanylar:
What was the film like? Was it worth seeing? - Kino nähili eken? Göreniňe degýärmidi?

I don't think newspapers are worth reading. Gazetlar okanyňa deger diýip oýlamaýaryn.

(Have) dificulty -ing

"Have difficulty doing something" ("to do" däl)- "Bir iş etmekde kynçylaga duş gelmek.

I had difficulty finding a place to live. Ýaşamana ýer tapmakda kynçylaga duş geldiňizmi? Did you have any difficulty getting a visa? Wiza almakda kynçylaga duş geldiňizmi?

People often have great difficulty reading my writing.-Meniň ýazuwymy okamakda adamlar kynçylyk çekýärler.

Adatça "have difficulty" ("have dificulties" däl) diýilýär.

I'm sure youll have no dificulty passing the exam. Seniň hiç hili kynçylyksyz synagdan geçjegiňe men ynanýaryn.

C

-ing aşakdakylardan soň ulanylýar.

a waste of money... /a waste of time... - pul ýitirmek ... / wagt ýitirmek ... - (to...-ny hem ulanmak mümkin)

It was a waste of time reading that book. –Şol kitaby okamak wagty biderek sarplamakdy.

It's a waste of money buying things you don't need. Geregi ýok zatlary satyn almak puly biderek sarplamakdyr.

spend/waste... sarplamakdyr / ýitirmek (wagt hakynda)

He spent hours trying to repair the clock. Ol sagady sazlamak üçin köp wagt ýitirdi.

I waste a lot of time daydreaming. Pikire çümüp köp wagt ýitirdim.

(be) busy.. - işli (bolmak):
She said she couldn't see me. She was too busy doing other things. – Ol meniň bilen görşüp bilmezligini aýtdy. Sebäbi ol başga zatlar bilen gaty işlidi.

Go swimming/ go fishing we ş.m

Go -ing birnäçe çäreler (esasanam sport) üçin ulanylýar:
go swimming/ go sailing/ go fishing/ go climbing/ go skiing we ş.m
şeýled-de: go shopping/ go sightseeing.

I’d like to go skiing. –Kankide typmana gitmek islärdim.

When did you last go shopping? Soňky gezek haçan dükanba söwda etmäge gitdiň?

Şeýle-de "come swimming/come skiing we ş.m " diýmek hem mümkin:
Why don't you come swimming with us? Näme üçin biz bilen suwada ýüzmänge barmaýarsyň?
