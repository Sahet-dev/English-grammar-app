Şu ýokardaky işlikler başga işliklerden ozal gelse adatça verb + to.. guluşy ulanylýar:
It was late, so we decided to take a taxi. Gaty giç bolupdy, şonuň üçin biz taksi saklamak kararyna geldik.

Simon was in a difficult situation, so l agreed to lend him some money. Simon agyr ýagdaýdady, şonuň üçin men oňa bir azajyk pul bermäne razy boldum.

How old were you when you learnt to drive? - Sürmäni öwreneniňde sen näçe ýaşdadyň?

I waved to Karen but failed to attract her attention. – Karena elimi galgadym emma men onuň ünsini çekip bilmedim

Aşakadaky not to... ulanylýan negatiw sözlemlere üns beriň:
We decided not to go because of the weather. – Howa ýagdaýy sebäpl biz barmazlyga karar etdik.

I promised not to be late. Men gijikmezlige wada berdim.

Köp işlikler bilen to... ulanylmaýar. mysal üçin enjoy/think/suggest:

I enjoy dancing. Men tans etmekden lezzetlenýärin. ("I enjoy to dance" däl)

Ian suggested going to the cinema. Aýen kino barmagy teklip etdi. ("suggested to go" däl)

Are you thinking of buying a car?- Maşyn almak barada pikir edýärsiňmi? ("thinking to buy" däl)

Şeýle dowa infinitiwi (to be doing) we perfect infinitiwi (to have done) bar:
I pretended to be reading the newspaper. Men özümi gazet okaýana aldym (okaýansyradym).

You seem to have lost weight. – Sen horlanana meňzýärsiň.

C

"Decide to do something", "promise to do something" ýaly birikmeler ulanylýar.

Şol sanda "a decision to do something" a promise to do something" (at + to...) ýaly birikmeler ulanylmagy mümkin:
I think his decision to give up his job was stupid.- Meniň pikirimçe onuň işini taşlamak karary akmaklykldy.

George has a tendency to talk too much. Jorjda juda köp geplemäge maýyllyk bar.

D

Dare işliginden soň infinitiwi to bilen hem, to-syz hem ulanmak mümkin:
I wouldn't dare to tell him. Ýa-da I wouldn't dare tell him. – Oňa aýtmana batyrynmazdym.

Emma negatiw(ýoklyk) daren't (ýa-da dare not) Formasyndan soň infinitiwi to-syz ulanylmagy dogry:
I daren't tell him what happened. – Oňa näme bolanyny aýdyp bermäge batyrynmaýaryn.

We asked how to get to the station. Biz stansiýa nädip barylýanyny soradyk.

Do you understand what to do? Näme etmelidigi düşünýärsiňmi?

Can anybody show me how to change the film on this camera? - Kimdir biri maňa bu fotoaparatda plýonkan nähili çalyşylýanyny görkezip bilermi?
