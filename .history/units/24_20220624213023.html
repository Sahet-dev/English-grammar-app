Will be doing
vs
will have done

A

Aşakdaky mysallary okaň:
Kevin loves football and this evening there is a big football match on television. The match begins at 7.30 and ends at 9.15. Paul wants to see Kevin the same evening and wants to know what time to come to his house.
Kewin futboly gaty gowy görýär we bugün agşam telewizorda möhim futbol oýny bolýar. Oýun sagat 7.30-da başlap, 9.15-da gutarýar. Pol Kewini şol agşam görmekçi bolýär we onuň öýe haçan gelýänini bilmekçi.

Paul: Is it all right if I come at about 8.30? Sagat 8.30-da barsam bolarmy?
Kevin: No, I'l be watching the football then. - Yok, men ol wagt futbol görüp oturan bolaryn.
Paul: Well, what about 9.30? -Onda, sagat 9.30-da näme?
Kevin: Fine. The match will have finished by then. Gayt gowy. Oýun oňa çenli tamamlanan bolar.

I will be doing something" (future continuous) - "Men bir iş edip ýören bolaryn. Futbol oýuny 7.30-dabaşlanyp, 9.15-da gutarýar. Diýmek, şol wagtyň dowamynda, mysal üçin 8.00-da Kewin oýun görüp oturan bolar - He will be watching the match.

Başga mysal:

I'm going on holiday on Saturday. This time next week I'll be lying on a beach or swimming in the sea. – Şenbe güni dynç alyşa çykýaryn. Geljek hepde şu wagt men plýajda ýatan ýa-da deňizde ýüzp ýören bolaryn.

Will be (do) + ing we will (do)-lary dňeşdiriň:

Don't phone me between 7 and 8. We'll be having dinner then. Sagat 7 bilen 8 aralygynda maňa jaň etme. Biz şol wagt agşamlyk nahar iýip oturan bolarys.

Let's wait for Mary to arrive and then we'll have dinner. Meriniň gelmegine garaşalyň, soňra biz agşamlyk nahar iýeris.

will be +ing -ini başga continuous formalar bilen deňeşdiriň:

At 10 d'clock yesterday, Sally was working. (past)-Düýn sagat 10-da Salli işlap ýördi.

It's 10 o'clock now. She is working. (present) - Häzir sagat 10. Ol işläp ýör.

At 10 o'clock tomorrow she will be working. (future)- Ertir sagat 10-da ol işläp ýören bolar.

C

Will be doing şeýlede geljekdäki tamamlanan iş-hereketi görkezmek üçin ulanykýar:

What time will your friends be arriving tomorrow? – Seniň doslaryň ertir nä wagtda ýetip gelen bolarlar?

Beýle mysallarda will be doing geljegi beýan edýän present continuous-a meňzeýär.

Will be doing -i biriniň etmekçi bolýan meýilleri barada soramak üçin ulanyp bolar, esasan bir zat islänimizde ýa-da kimdir biriniň bi iş etmegini isleseňiz. Mysal üçin:

A: Will you be using your bike this evening? Bugün agşam welosipediňi ulanarsyňmy?

B: No. Do you want to borrow it? - Yok. Ony ulanmaga almakçy bolrýarsyňmy?

D

Will have (done) (future perfect) bir iş-hereketiň mälim wagta çenli tamamlanjakdygy görkezmek üçin ulanylýar Kewiniň görýän futbol oýuny 9.15-da tamamlanýar, diýmek şol wagtdan soň mysal üçin 9.30-da oýun gutaran bolar. The match will have finished.

Başga mysal: We're late. The film will already have started by the time we get to the cinema. - Biz gijikýäris. Kinoteatra barýançak yetib kino eýýam başlanan bolar.

Will have done-y başga perfect formalar bilen deňeşdiriň:

Ted and Amy have been married for 24 years. (present perfect) - Ted we Emi 24 ýyldan bäri är-aýal.

Next year they will have been married for 25 years. (future perfect) – Geljek ýyl olaryň är-aýal bolanyna 25 ýyl bolar.

When their first child was born, they had been married for three years. (past perfect) Olaryň birinji bäbegi doglanda olar üç ýyldan bäri är-aýal bolandylar.
