vs
I did

A

Aşakdaky mysaly öwreniň:

Tom açary gözläp ýör. Ol ony tapyp bilenak.
He has lost his key. (present perfect)- Ol açary ýitiren. Bu "häzir onda açr ýok” diýen manyny aňladýar.

On minut soňra:

Now Tom has found his key. He has it now. Tom açary tapdy. Häzir onda açar bar.

Has he lost his key? (present perfect) -Ol açary ýitirenmi?

No, he hasn't. He has found it. - Ýok, ol ony tapdy.

Did he lose his key? (past simple) -Ol açary ýitirdimi?

Yes, he did. - Howwa.



He lost his key (past simple) but now he has found it. (present perfect) -Ol açary ýitiripdi, ýöne häzir ol ony tapdy.

Present perfect- häzirki zamandyr. Ol hemişe häzirki zaman barada maglumat berýär. "Tom has lost his key"- "Tom açary ýitiren" "Onda häzir açar ýok.

Past simple diňe geçen zaman hakda maglumat berýär. "Tom lost his key" Diýlende häzir onda açar barmy ýa-da ýoklyygyny bilip bolmaýar. Diňe onuň geçmişdäki nahilidir bir wagtda açary ýitirendigi belli.

Ýene şuňa meňzeş mysallar.

Jack grew a beard but now he has shaved it off.- Jek sakal ösdüripdi, ýöne häzir ol ony aýryp taşlan. (diýmek, häzir onda sakal ýok)

They went out after lunch and they've just come back. Günertanlyk nahardan soňra olar daşary gidipdiler, olar häzirjek dolandylar. (diýmek, gaýdanlar)

Shakespeare wrote many plays. Şekspir Köp pieseler ýazypdyr.

My sister is a writer. She has written many books. Meniň doganym ýazyjy. Ol köp kitaplar ýazan. (ol häzire çenli kitap ýazan)

A: Ow! I've burnt my hand. – Wah, elimi ýakdym.

B: How did you do that? – Nädip boldy?

A: I picked up a hot dish. – Gyzgyn gazany tutdumy.
