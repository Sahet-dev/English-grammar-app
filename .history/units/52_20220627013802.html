ver + doing
(enjoy doing, stop doing)

A

Aşakdaky mysallary öwreniň:
Would you mind closing the door? –Gapyny ýapyp göýberersiňizmi? ("mind to close" däl)

I enjoy dancing. - Men tans oýnamakdan lezzet alýaryn. ("I enjoy to dance" däl)

I suggested going to the cinema. – Men kino barmagy teklip etdim. ("suggested to go däl)

Enjoy, mind we suggest-den soň gelýän işlige +ing goşulýar (to... däl).

+ing bilen ulanylýan ýene bir näçe işlikleri sanawy:
stop

fancy
consider
admit
miss
involve
finish
postpone
imagine
avoid
deny
risk
practise

Suddenly everybody stopped talking. Birden hemmeler gürlemekden saklandy.

Have you finished cleaning the room? Sen otagy arassalap bolduňmy?

He tried to avoid answering my question. – Ol meniň soragyma jogap bermezlige çalyşdy.

Have you ever considered going to live in another country?- Haçandyr bir wagt daşary ýurtda ýaşamak barada pikir edip gördüňmi?

Ýoklyk formasyna not +ing bolýandygyna üns beriň:
When I'm on holiday, I enjoy not having to get up early.- Men dynç alyşda ir turmagym zerur däldiginden rahatlanýaryn.

B

Aşakdakylardan soň + ing ulanylýar:
give up (= stop) taşlamak, saklamak
put off (= postpone) – galdyrmak, soňa goýmak
carry on / go on (= continue) – dowam etmek
keep ýa-da keep on - dowam etdip gelmek (bir zady etmekde)
Paula has given up smoking. – Paula çekmegi taşlady.
We must do something. We can't go on living like this! Bir zat etmegimiz zerur, biz beýle ýaşamagy dowam edip bilmeris!

C

Birnäçe işlikler bilen işlik + ing gurluşy ulanmak mümkin:
I can't imagine George riding a motorbike. Jorjyň motosikl sürýänini göz öňüne getirip bilemak.

You can't stop me doing what I want. – Meni islänimi etmekden saklap bilmersiň.

Don't keep me waiting!- Meni garaşdyryp goýma!

Passive formasyna (being done/seen/kept we ş.m) üns beriň: I don't mind being kept waiting. Meni garaşdyryp goýanyň zyýany ýok.(nägile bolmaýaryn). (= I don't mind people keeping me..)

D

Tamamlanan iş-hereket barada gürrüň edilende having done/stolen/said we ş.m ulanylmagy mümkin:
She admitted having stolen the money. Ol puly ogurlanyny boýun aldy.

Emma having (done)- i ulanmak şert däl, tamamlana iş-hereket üçin diňe –ing-ni hem ulanmak mümkin:
She admitted stealing the money. - Ol puly ogurlanyny boýun aldy.

I now regret saying (ýa-da having said) what I said. Indi aýdanlarymy aýdanym üçin ökünýärin.

Regret Unit 55 B-de.

E

Şu sahypadaky käbir işliklerden(esasan admit/ deny/suggest) soň that-y ulanmak mümkin:
She denied that she had stolen the money. (ýa-da She denied stealing.)- Ol puly ogurlanyny inkär etdi.

Ian suggested that we went to the cinema. (ýa-da Ian suggested going...) - Aýen kino gitmegi teklip etdi.
