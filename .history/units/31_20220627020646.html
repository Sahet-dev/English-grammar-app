must
have to

A

Must we have to bir iş-hereketi ýerine ýetirrmek hökmanydygyny görkezmek üçin ulanylýar. Käte olaryň haýsy birini ulanýanymyzyň tapawudy hem galmaýar:
Oh, it's later than I thought. I must go. (ýa-da I have to go)- Men oýlanymdan giç. Gitmegim zerur.

Emma must we have to –nyň arasynda tapawut bar, ol käwagt möhümdir.

Must şahsy duýgyny, pikirler bildirlende ulanylýar.

Have to biziň şahsy duýgularymyzyy we pikirleri dälde, faktlary bildirmek üçin ulanylýar.

"You must do something" = "sen bir iş etmegiň zerur, men muny möhüm diýýänim üçin etmegiň zerur"

She's a really nice person. You must meet her. – Ol örän ajaýyp adam. Sen hökman onuň bilen tanyşmaly. (meniň pikirim)

I’haven't phoned Ann for ages. I must phone her tonight. Men Ena köpden bäri jaň etmedim. Men oňa agşam hökman jaň etmeli.

You have to do something" = " Sen bir zady düzgün bolany üçin etmeli ýa-da ýagdaýy talap edýäni üçin etmegiň zerur.

You have to turn left here. You can't turn right here. You have to turn left. Sen bu yerden saga öwrülip bilmeýärsiň. Sen hökman çepe öwrülmeli.(ýol hereketiniň düzgünine laýyklykda)

My eyesight isn't very good. I have to wear glasses for reading. – Meniň gşzim dury däl. Okamagym üçin men hökman äýnej dakmaly.

Aşakdakylary deňeşdiriň:
I must get up early tomorrow. There are a lot of things I want to do. Men ertir ir bilen turmagym zerur. Men etmek isleýän köp işler bar.

I have to get up early tomorrow. I'm going away and my train leaves at 7.30. – Men ertir ir turmagym zerur. Men gidýärin we meniň otlym sagat 7.30-da ugraýar.

Haçanda haýsyny ulanmany bilmedik ýagdaýňyzda have to-ny ulanmak maksada laýyk.

B

Must geçen zamanda ulanylmaýar, eýsem häzirki we geljek zamanda ulanylýar:
We must go now. – Biz häzir barmagynyz zerur.

We must go tomorrow. – Biz ertir barmagynyz zerur . (emma "We must go yesterday" däl)

Have to-ny hemme formalarda ulanmak mümkin. Mysal üçin:
I had to go to hospital. – Biz hassahana gitmegimiz zerur boldy. (past)

Have you ever had to go to hospital? - Hassahana gitmeli bolup gördüňizmi? (present perfect)

I might have to go to hospital. Men hassahan zerur bolmagy mümkin.

(might-dan soň infinitiv)
Sorag we ýoklyk aňladýan sözlemlerde have to bilen bile adatça do/does/did ulanylýar:
What do I have to do to get a driving licence? - Men sürüjilik şahadatnamasyny almak üçin näm etmegim zerur?

Why did you have to go to hospital?- Hassahan näme üçin gitmegiň zerur bolupdy?

Karen doesn't have to work on Saturdays. Karen Şenbe günleri işlemegi zerur däl.

C

Mustn't we don't have to tapwutlydyr:

"You mustn't do something" = "bir işi etmezligi şert (şonuň üçin ony etme)"

"You don't have to do something" = " bir işi etmegiň zeur däl (ýöne isleseň edip bilersiň)"

You must keep it a secret. You mustn't tell anyone. Sen muny syr saklamagyň zerur. Hiç kime aýtmazlygyň zerur.

You can tell me if you want but you don't have to tell me. Eger isleseň maňa aýdyp bilersiň. Ýöne aýtmagyň zerur däl.

D

Have to -nyň ýerine have got to-y ulanmak mümkin:
I've got to work tomorrow. Ýa-da I have to work tomorow. – Ertir işlemegim zerur.
