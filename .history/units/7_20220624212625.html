Aşakdaky myssallary okaň

Tom is looking for his key. He can't find it. He has lost his key.
Tom açaryny gözleýär. Ony tapyp bilenak. Ol ony ýitiripdi.

"He has lost his key" = "Ol açaryny ýitiripdi (Onda açarlar ýok)".

Have/has lost - present perfect'-iň ýasalyş formasy:

I/ we/ they/ you have (I’ve/we’ve/ they’ve / you’ve/ we ş.m) | finishedl/ lost/ passed he/she/it has (he's / she's/ it's we ş.m) been/done we ş.m

Present perfect zamanynda nädogry işlikleriň üçinji görnüşi ulanylýar (lost/ done/been/written).

"Açaryň nirede?" "Bilemak. Ony ýitirdim" (häzir mende açar ýok)

He told me his name but l've forgotten it. –
Ol maňa adyny aýdypdy, emma men ony unudypdyryn. (Häzir onuň ady ýadymda ýok)

"Is Sally here?" "No, she's gone out."
- "Salli şu ýerdemi?" "Yok, çykyp giden" (häzir ol ýok, daşarda)

I can't find my bag. Have you seen it?
- Men sumkamni tapyp bilemak. Ony görmediňizmi? (siz häzir onuň nirdedigini bilýärsiňizmi?)

Present perfect täze maglumat bermek ýa-da ýakynda bolup geçen wakany mälim etmek üçin ulanylýar:
