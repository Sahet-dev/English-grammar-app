58.1 Haýsy birini makul bilýärsiňiz? I prefer (something) to (something else) –i ulanyp gürrüňler ýazyň. Gerekli ýerler üçin işligi dogry forma geçiriň:

1.(drive/travel by train) I prefer driving to travelling by train.

2. (tennis/football) I prefer

3. (phone people/write letters) I to

4. (go to the cinema /watch films on TV)
Indi, I prefer (to do something) gurluşyny ulanyp 3 we 4-nji gürrüňleri gaýtadan ýazyň.

5. (1) I prefer to drive rather than travel by train.

6. (3) I prefer to

7.(4)

58.2 I'd prefer... ýa-da l'd rather... bilen aşakdakylardan birini ulanyp sözlemler ýazyň:

eat at home
get a taxi
go alone
go for a swim
listen to some music
stand
think about it for a while
wait a few minutes
wait till later
1. Shall we walk home? (prefer)
I'd prefer to get a taxi

2. Do you want to eat now? (rather)
I’d rather wait till later

3. Shall we watch TV? (prefer)

4. What about a game of tennis?
(rather)

5. Shall we leave now? (rather)

6. Do you want to go to a restaurant?
(prefer)

7.I think we should decide now?
(rather)

8. Would you like to sit down?
(rather)

9. Do you want me to come with you?
(prefer)

Indi, than we rather than –y ulanyp sözlemler ýazyň

10. (get a taxi/ walk home)

I'd prefer to get a taxi rather than walk home.

11.(go for a swim/ play tennis) I'd rather

12. (wait a few minutes/ leave now) l'd rather

13. (eat at home / go to a restaurant) l'd prefer

14. (think about it for a while / decide now) I'd rather

58.3 Would you rather I –y ulanyp gürrüňleri dolduryň

1. Are you going to cook the dinner or would you rather I cooked it?

2. Are you going to tell Ann what happened or would you rather ?

3. Are you going to do the shopping or ?

4. Are you going to answer the phone or ?

58.4 Pikirlenip sözlemleri dolduryň

1. "Shall l tell Ann the news?" "No, l'd rather she didn't know.

2. Do you want me to go now or would you rather here?

3. Do you want to go out this evening or would you rather at home?

4. This is a private letter addressed to me. l'd rather you read it.

5. It's quite a nice house but l'd rather it a bit bigger.

6. "Do you mind if I turn on the radio?" "I’d rather you . I'm trying to study." .
