51.2 Wakalary okaň we mini-soraglar ulanyp sözlemler ýazyň. Her bir waka üçin dostuňyzdan siziň pikiriňize goşulmagyny soraň.

1. You look out of the window. The sky is blue and the sun is shining. What do you say to your friend? (beautiful day)
It's a beautiful day, isn't it?

2. You're with a friend outside a restaurant.
You're looking at the prices, which are very high. What do you say?
(expensive) It

3.
You've just come out of the cinema with a friend. You really enjoyed the film. What do you say to your friend?
(great) The film

4. You and a friend are listening to a woman singing. You like her voice very much. What do you say to your friend?
(a lovely voice) She

5. You are trying on a jacket. You look in the mirror and you don't like what you see. What do you say to your friend?
(not / look/very good) It

6. Your friend's hair is much shorter than when you last met. What do you say to her/him?
(have/your hair/ cut) You

7. You and a friend are walking over a wooden bridge. It is very old and some parts are broken. What do you say?
(not/very safe) This bridge
