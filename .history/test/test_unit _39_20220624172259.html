2. Ken got to the station in time to catch his train. If ___________ ( he/miss) it, _______________ (he/be) late for his interview

3. It's good that you reminded me about Ann's birthday ____________ (I/forget) if __________ (you/not/remind) me.

4. Unfortunately, I didn't have my address book with me when I was in New York. If ____________ (I/have) your address, ______________ (I/send) you a postcard.

5. A: How was your holiday? Did you have a nice time?
B: It was OK, but __________ (we/enjoy) it more if ___________ (the weather/be) better.

6. I took a taxi to the hotel but the traffic was very bad. ___________ (it/be) quicker if ___________ I/walk).
