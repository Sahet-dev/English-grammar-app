This was very early for him. When Juan first came to England, he _________ dinner so early, but after some time he _________ it. Now he finds it quite normal. He _________ at six o'clock. 3. Julia is a nurse. A year ago she started working nights. At first she found it hard. At first Julia didn't like it. She ________ nights and it took her a few months to __________ it.
